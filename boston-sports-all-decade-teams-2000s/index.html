// Boston Sports All-Decade Teams: 2000’s

The 2000’s. The Aughts. The greatest decade in the history of Boston sports. Possibly…the greatest decade for any city in the history of sports. Six championships. Three teams. Enough with my fragmented sentences.

Sports of Boston has put together a list of the greatest players of each of the four major teams (Red Sox, Patriots, Celtics, Bruins) this decade. You may or may not agree with our picks, and there were some tough cuts. You think you can do better? Let us know!
