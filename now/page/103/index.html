These days, it appears Tom Brady is doing a better job cementing his legacy as a hilarious quarterback than a Super Bowl-winning one.

Teaming up with CVS Pharmacy and the makers of the new Unreal Candy, Brady stars in a series of epic staring contest showdowns with unsuspecting CVS customers. Of course, the highlight of the commercial is when Brady is cuffed and shoved into the back of a police squad car for handing out candy to kids from the front seat of a tinted SUV.
