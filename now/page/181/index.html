Book Review – Delivering Happiness: A Path to Profits, Passion and Purpose

June 7, 2010 at 10:58pm in Featured, Now!, Red Sox
Mike Carlucci writes
Add a comment

Cowboy up! The Idiots. Tessie. Pink hats. Red Sox Nation. These mottos have defined the relationship between the Boston Red Sox and their fans. A relationship that has had its share of highs and lows, taking the fans and the team to new places in ways neither party thought could be possible. For better or worse, the Red Sox are more than a baseball team theses days. They are a well-oiled baseball machine, a culture, and a corporation with essentially one goal: keeping the fans happy.

A Tale of Two Industries

In Delivering Happiness: A Path to Profits, Passion and Purpose, Tony Hsieh, CEO of Zappos takes readers on a journey through his life and the experiences that brought him to the online shoe store (Zappos.com) as well as the philosophies that guided the company from the Internet boom of the late 90s to its successful sale to Amazon in 2009. (Full disclosure, I received an advanced copy free of charge). As he guided Zappos from scrappy startup to household name, Hsieh (pronounced Shay) realized that business success wasn’t just about sales and product and personnel. These are all important, but having a unified company culture and a focus beyond just staying in business and profitability is just as essential to a company’s success. Customer service is the central pillar of Zappos’ business strategy.

Ditto for the undisputed princes of the league, the Yankees. The NL isn’t as clear cut, with everyone going nuts on each other; the East is led by 2.0 games, the Central is tied, and the West is led by 0.5 games. What does that mean for our power rankings? It means three new teams have appeared, and another is knocking on the doorstep. Lamar Odom’s Wife Khloe Kardashian Pregnant?

June 3, 2010 at 12:20pm in Celtics, Now!
KC Downey writes
One comment

In news more fit for a gossip blog than a sports blog, Lamar Odom’s wife, Khloe Kardashian (taller, more awkard sister of babe Kim Kardashian), may be expecting the couple’s first child. If you watched any of the “Keeping up with the Kardashians” episodes, you obviously knew that Khloe was jealous of her sister Kourtney’s pregnancy and wanted a baby of her own. So, perhaps she’s getting her wish.

OK, so how do I know that? That’s besides the point.

The news is of course relevant to Boston sports, as Lamar Odom and the Lakers are set to duke it out with the Celtics on Thursday night in Game 1 of the NBA Finals. Athletes typically perform well to positive baby news, so if it’s true and not another hot rumor, Odom could be in line for 15 points and 10 boards off the bench in Game 1. Stay tuned!
