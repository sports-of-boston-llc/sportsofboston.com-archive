When we were little kids, we’d always pretend to make the game-ending, home run-robbing catch over the centerfield wall. (Well, maybe that was just me, in which case I’m using the royal “we.”)

The tournament is held at Little Fenway, a to-scale version of Fenway Park in Middle-of-Nowhere, Vermont (I’m pretty sure that’s what I entered in the GPS the one time I went there), and helps raise money to enhance the lives of those with spinal cord injuries and their families.
