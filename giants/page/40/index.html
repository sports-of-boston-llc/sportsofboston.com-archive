Charlie Whitehurst Too Much As New York Giants Lose At Home 36-25

October 10, 2011 at 12:37am in Featured, Giants
Gabriel Yanez writes
One comment

It looked like yet another fourth quarter win for the New York Giants, but this week’s effort came up short against some unlikely heroes for the Seattle Seahawks, who defeated the Giants at MetLife Stadium 36-25.

Heroics from the Seattle Bench

Seattle’s starting quarterback, Tarvaris Jackson was knocked out of the game in the third quarter and replacement, Charlie Whitehurst, picked up right where Jackson left off. With Justin Tuck on the bench, the Giants defense failed to consistently put pressure on the quarterback, allowing Jackson and then Whitehurst to pass for a combined 315 yards.
