Connelly’s Top Ten – Gronk is Broke, Horford not Broke, Brady Doesn’t Choke

By Michael in Uncategorized ⋅ December 1, 2016 at 7:38pm ⋅ Post a comment

1.Patriots vs. Rams

*Gronk hurt again – ugh. This season is following last year’s path – they started 10-0 and finished 3-5
* Goodell’s whole week was made with this injury
*Tom Brady is 1,132 yards short of passing Dan Marino for 4th on all time passing yards (behind Manning, Favre and Brees)
* A question – has Charlie Weis, Josh McDaniel’s game plans and Tom Brady’s skill set been a cause for getting a lot of receivers / tight ends hurt with the short game, quick hitters over the middle, etc?
* Rams – my favorite player growing up was Roman Gabriel / Jack Youngblood played with broken leg / old blue and white helmets were awesome
* Jeff Fisher used to be so smug – now just a loser
2. Nothing to do with the maternity leave – but it’s obvious that Tommy Heinsohn was right about Al Horford he is averaging– 14 points / 6.6 rebounds for $26mm a year (a year, a year) – is he better than players on the below list – considering he got the biggest contract in CELTIC HISTORY:
Sullinger – 2014 – 13.3 points per game / 8.1 rebounds per game
Potapenko – 1999 – 10.8 points per game / 7.2 rebounds per game
Pickney……1989 – 10.1 points per game / 5.1 rebounds per game
Rowe…… ..1977 – 10.1 points per game / 7.1 rebounds per game
Wicks……..1977 – 15.1 points per game / 10.0 rebounds per game
Radja………1996 – 19.7 points per game / 9.8 rebounds per game
LaFrentz…..2005 – 11.1 points per game / 6.9 rebounds per game
3. Bruins / Celtics
* Again – remember when media used to be tough? Fluto Shinzawa enabling again in Globe piece – Bergeron “has fought through bad luck” (3 goals in 20 games) WTF! “Krejci wheels remain a work in progress” – WTF???? (3 goals in 23 games) – so SIX goals in 46 games for combined salaries of $14mm
* Right now – the Brooklyn pick this year would be the third pick
* Celtics can swap with Nets this year (2018 they have Nets and Grizzlies first pick)
4. Around College Football
* Navy has two games to jump two spots to catch Western Michigan to get a Cotton Bowl bid -wow – that will first big bowl since 1963 (someone should write a book)!
* No lock – but I like under in W. Virginia – Baylor game 68.5
* A good Oklahoma State team getting 11.5 in a rivalry game – hmm – I take the points
* My weekly over/under on Navy – go over on the total 61 against Temple
* I like Wisconsin giving 2.5 to Penn State
* I like San Diego State getting 6.5 in Wyoming
5. From Miguel’s Patriots salary cap page –
* Alan Branch really must love pot – for the four games he is going to miss for suspension he will lose $1,132,353 in salary and playing time bonuses
* Total salaries on injured lists $8.7mm…………Vollmer is $5.2 himself
* Players over $5mm  on the salary cap– Brady, Solder, Hightower, Sheard, Gronk, Hogan, Bennett, McCourty
* Players not on the team impacting the cap – $18mm – Mayo is $4.4mm / Easley $2.9mm / Cooper $1.9mm / Scott Chandler $2mm
6. Brady giving up Pick Sixes:
* Only twelve in his career – over 8,048 pass attempts – that’s a pick six every 671 attempts
* Geno Smith has eight over his career – over 866 attempts – that’s a pick six every 108 attempts
* Top five in Pick Sixes – Favre – 31 / Marino – 28 / Manning – 26 / Brees – 25 / Testeverde -23
* Ty Law had one of Brady’s / two of Mannings / one of Warner’s – in the Super Bowl
* Bledsoe had 15 / Plunkett – 12 / Grogan – 11
* John Brodie two over 4,491 passes – one every 2,246 passes / Staubach had two over 2,958

9 Just a Little Bit Outside – American casualties at Battle of the Bulge – Doing some math 81,000 casualties divided by 50 states = 1,620 per state – Massachusetts has 312 towns which means every town would have had 5 casualties from just one battle – and people are burning flags that they fought for.
10. Randomocity
* Great documentary of Canada – Russia 1972 series on Youtube: “Cold War on ice summit series HD”
* Its amazing to think of the abuse Jack Bicknell took in this town after Flutie left compared to the free ride Adazzio gets from the media and school – for $2.6mm a year
*The girl who inspired My Sharona song by the Knack is a prominent LA realtor now and her website has the song playing the background
* George Blanda played in the NFL when Harry Truman was president and Gerald Ford
* Life is good – Tiger Woods back
