Connelly’s Top Ten – Season is Over and Window is CLOSING!

By Michael in Uncategorized ⋅ November 27, 2016 at 7:54pm ⋅ Post a comment

1.Patriots vs. Jets

 

MISC

 

·         Season is over, they have no offensive creativity (Except one double pass), Gronk hurt, Brady really hurt, Bennett hurt, defense one of the worst in the last fifteen years – can’t pressure QB or cover anyone – its over. Sorry!

·         Gotta love them picking on Revis for the game winner

·         Off to another frustrating start – Brady hit the first two plays / doesn’t get play off on time giving Jets chance to challenge their first down and it is reversed / Have to punt getting Jordan Richards hurt (you could hear him yell)

 

BRADY

 

·         He has a bad injury that will need surgery at some point – might need to rest him

·         Ball was sailing high on him because he can’t put pressure on knee

·         Brady completely Andy Reid-ed the end of the half – what a butcher job – with 1:16 left in the half he completes pass to Amendola, doesn’t use timeout – then doesn’t get off a play until 0:45 (36 seconds!), then completes pass to Hogan at 0:40 and again doesn’t use a timeout – throws a desperation pass to Mitchell at 0:06 that’s incomplete – they have to settle for field goal which they miss – and they take the timeout into the locker room

·         Do not put the Brady blocking downfield on the – “How To Block” – video

·         Brady with over three seconds doesn’t work again – overthrows an open Gronk and gets him hurt

·         He looked grumpy like he knew they were in big trouble

 

OFFENSE

 

·         Josh McDaniel – UGHHHH! His schemes are so vanilla – how many plays drawn up allow a defender to run free to Brady?

·         NO HURRY UP – not even at the end of the first half when it was hurry up time

·         Again they tried the play-action, fake end around – and again nothing

·         Thank God for Malcom Mitchell

·         Gutsy call going for it on 4th and 4 – and not giving Gostkowski a chance

·         Do I get any points for calling the left handed Hogan option pass a month ago?

·         Nice downfield blocking by Mitchell and Hogan on the Blount 18 yard run

·         Every time Lewis touches the ball it looks like he is just ready to be knocked out for the season

·         4-14 on third down – not good

 

DEFENSE

 

·         Everytime the Patriots go with a three man rush I should say an Act of Contrition for what I’m thinking

·         The lack of pass rush, an average linebacker corps is not good enough to win a Super Bowl I’m sorry to say – watching McClellin is like watching a Tufts football player

·         How come Matt Patricia turns every average quarterback into Sid Luckman (Fitzpatrick had a QB rating of 67.6 coming into the game and goes off for 115.2)

·         Malcom Brown a little better – put pressure on Fitzpatrick forcing an intentional grounding

·         Butler’s worst game as a Patriot – was getting beat all over the field

·         Ninkovich is proving the value of steroids – he is a JV player right now – 1 tackle

·         How does Van Noy get matched up one on one with CJ Spiller down the right sideline?

 

BELICHICK GARD / KRAFT SIGHTINGS

 

·         Bill same gray hoodie from the Simpson’s Boston special

·         Kraft 6:24 left in the 2nd quarter

 

INJURIES

 

·         Gronk yet again – I would not sign him for crazy money – he has a body of a 42-year-old (same about Edelman – don’t sign for big money slowly declining)

·         Bennett – that ankle is going to be a problem all year

·         Richard injury I spoke of already

 

MISC

 

·         Gostkowski another miss – yikes – I think it’s worse than that. His body language is not good and the ball is coming off his foot funny almost knuckling

·         Saw it in the Michigan game and every football game – the marking of the football is so random – you need technology to step in

·         Greg Gumbel brings nothing to the table but stating the obvious – and to think that CBS named him their number one announcer over Sean McDonough fifteen years ago

·         The league is imparting parity upon the Patriots – Take away two first round picks, suspend the best player in the league, no fine on defenders taking shots at the best player in the league’s knee which could change the course of the season, and the refusal to call interference on players defending against Gronk

 

2. Around the NFL

 

·         Article about Matt Forte deciding on the Jets over the Patriots in the offseason – that wasn’t the best career decision

·         I feel this about changing pitchers and people – if you think should make a change – then make the change – Cincy embarrasses itself last year in the playoffs and they come back with Marvin Lewis and they are 3-7-1

·         Drew Brees 458 TD to 213 INT / Brady 446 to 151 INT

·         Carson Palmer 4-6-1 since talking bad about Brady 

·         Jamie Collins 0-3 now

·         Its approaching December when Chandler Jones goes into hibernation (or to the police station)– 1 tackle – that is not Von Miller- like

·         Ravens run a play in which they commit a penalty on purpose so that they play is re-run and the clock runs again – doesn’t seem like the integrity of the game – ie – tackle eligible

·         I think domes should be outlawed – boring inside game that is sterile

·         Tony Romo salary cap dollar figure $14,000,000 / Dak Prescott salary $450,000

 

3. Bruins / Celtics

 

·         Celtics 2-4 against teams with an above .500 record / 7-3 versus losing teams

·         Bruins 5-9 against teams with an above .500 record / 7-1 versus losing teams

 

4. Around College Football

* I was 1-2 ugh

* I agree with Harbaugh – biggest game of the year and the home team gets penalized SIX yards to opponents 59 yards including three questionable interference calls and an insane bench penalty 50 yards away from the play never mind having the benefit of the spots all day

* I wonder what the break down on subjective interference calls are on home team versus the away teams

* The eight-team playoff would make lots of millions – first round: Alabama vs. Penn State / Ohio State vs. Oklahoma / Clemson vs. Wisconsin / Michigan vs. Washington

* With so many fans storming the fields now – there must be at least one fan breaking an ankle a game jumping over the walls

* With the coaching changes in college football – seniors should be allowed to transfer to another team – not the team that the coach went to though

* I don’t get it – Boston College is excited about their season and becoming bowl eligible? They only played four teams in the top 60 (according to RPI) which they were 0-4 against while being outscored 202-21! Remember when the media in this town mattered? Addazio said after the game: “top of our university, unwavering faith”

 

5. In 2018 the Red Sox have already committed $108mm to five players – Price, Sandoval, Hanley, Pedroia and Porcello.

 

6. I wish USA Soccer was paying attention to my blog back in June (6/24/16) when I showed that Klinsmann wasn’t improving the team and that their best coach over the last twenty years has been Arena – it’s just that he doesn’t look the part – more of a John Lovitz wanna be.

* FIFA Ranking over the six years since Klinsmann took over in 2011 – 34 / 2012 – 28 / 2013- 28 / 2014 – 13 / 2015 – 27 / 2016 – 31st  

* Average FIFA Rankings for the USA’s last four coaches – Arena (98-06) –  17th / Sampson (95-98) – 22nd / Bradley (06-11) – 23rd / Klinsmann (11-16) – 28th

7 Old School – Prior to Carlton Fisk home run in Game 6 of 1975 World Series – he had four over his previous 156 at bats – or one every 39 at bats

 

8 Amazing Stat – Cam Neely scored 50 goals in 44 games in 1993-1994 but on one leg. After scoring his 50th he only played six more games and missed the playoffs – Niedermeyer dead, Wormer dead, Ulf – dead! 

 9 Just a Little Bit Outside – Local Sports anchor salaries in 1986 – (Globe)

Bob Lobel………$450,000

Mike Lynch……..$200,000

John Dennis…….$140,000

 

10.  Randomocity

* What’s wrong with our society? With 21 more shot in Chicago Saturday – TV is advertising – in the middle of the day mind you – a video game from Playstation called WatchDogs 2 which shows street thugs with machine guns shooting people in the middle of a city further romanticizing the greatest plague ever to hit the United States – insane

* After Cam Newton refused to pick up the fumble in the Super Bowl shouldn’t the DAB go away

* The owner of Corona just went back to his village in Spain – Cerezales del Coronado and made every citizen a millionaire   

* Castro must have been a big Brady Bunch fan

* Boston College is selling sports tickets for 50% off on Monday – hate to be that season ticket holder that paid full price and has been loyal despite the bad product

* Sports Illustrated – two recent NBA articles – Are the Bulls Better Off Without Rondo? AND Buyer’s Remorse – top five bad signings – first two are Evan Turner and Rajon Rondo

* Hats off to U Mass Boston and Charlie Titus for not allowing two of their stars play in the NCAA playoffs after their disgraceful red cards two weeks ago

* You can’t have Frosty the Snowman and Rudolph on before December 1st
