Connelly’s Top Ten: Missed Extra Point, O Line Whiffs, McDaniels Throws Up!

By Michael in Featured, Now!, Top Story ⋅ January 25, 2016 at 7:35am ⋅ Post a comment

1. PATRIOTS GET OUTCOACHED IN DENVER

*No one to blame but themselves for not having home field advantage – Philly – Pats handed them game / Miami that stupid running game plan / Jets outhit them / Drop punt in Denver

* Biggest failures in the game – missed extra point (hasn’t missed since 2006) / O Line / McDaniel got his ass kicked by Phillips

* Cincy hands game to Pitt / Pitt hands game to Denver / Pat’s hand home field and AFC championship and bragging rites to Manning

PREGAME

  • Kraft sighting – double kiss for Brady
  • 13 degrees warmer in Denver than Foxboro – no one to blame but themselves
  • Good national anthem – nice they showed it
  • WTF – They win the toss and take the ball against the best defense and loudest crowd – why change now and not defer?
  • CBS stat – When Brady is sacked more than twice in playoffs 2-3 / 2 or less 20-5

FIRST QUARTER

  • Butler not dancing this game getting beat often – why ten yard cushion on 3rd and ten
  • Ed Hoculi going with lefty first down call
  • Bill in Blue wind breaker
  • Not sure if London is happy with movie London is Falling
  • Are you kidding me life time criminal Frank Abagnale from Catch Me If You Can gets an endorsement from IBM
  • Throwing up on themselves – Stork head butt for 15 yards – kills a drive
  • Like the Navy commercials
  • With the new receiver rules – receivers look for calls on every play
  • Brady is in one of those bitchy moods – no intensity
  • Refs – didn’t want to call that backwards pass a fumble
  • Spear on Bolden has to be a bigger fine than Amendola’s legal hit
  • Throwing up on themselves – Gostkowski misses his first extra point

SECOND QUARTER

  • Kline getting destroyed
  • Brady not stepping into throw for interception by Von Miller
  • Tablets not working on Patriot side – thank God we’re not in Foxboro
  • Bad commercial for Microsoft
  • How come Brady is so confused / how come no hurry up / how come every pattern is 4 seconds
  • Did LaFell make the trip
  • Surprised James White didn’t have to go through concussion protocol
  • No creativity – no screens, end arounds, double passes, hurry up, anything!!!!
  • Another penalty for Stork
  • Gronk jumping offsides
  • Are you f-ing kidding me back to back runs to Bolden up the middle
  • Brady getting hit every time – Manning once
  • Jamie Collins worst half as a Patriot burnt twice for touchdowns
  • Chandler Jones still outside Foxboro police station looking for his shirt?
  • Horse crap punt by Allen sets up Denver field goal at end of half –
  • McDaniel getting his ass kicked by Wade Phillips
  • Patriots hand Denver the sandwich possessions around half time by taking the ball with coin win
  • What kind of week of preparation did they have – no answers

3RD QUARTER

  • Temp in Foxboro at start of the half 29 / Denver 46
  • Bolden has three carries for 4 yards
  • Marcus Cannon worst offensive line man in NFL history?
  • Von Miller grabbing crotch after sack
  • Fleming had one job as extra offensive line man and whiffed
  • Kraft at 6:42 of third quarter
  • Gloria Swanson in Denver owner’s box
  • Brady has to switch up cadence or something (if they are in Foxboro no crowd noise to worry about)
  • Where is the hurry up?
  • Gronk can’t be hyperventilating on sideline with team in red zone settling for three points
  • HGH and Manning finally mentioned with 8:00 left in third quarter
  • Brady short arming some balls
  • Giselle is yelling – “he can’t throw and block”
  • Patriots sideline is dead flat – can’t believe it – they look intimidated
  • Every pattern outside numbers looked strange with multiple Patriot receivers in the same area
  • No QB in NFL history took a beating like this and kept getting up – stay down Rocky!

4th QUARTER

* Temp in Foxboro 43 – Denver 27

* Butler had two hands on last Denver’s last play could have intercepted the ball

* Crazy how Stewart and Keo were head hunting

* Gronk held by Talib like Kuechly did in Carolina (Talib finally didn’t wimp out and limp off field in an AFC Championship game)

* Brady missed James White twice the sideline for TD’s

* Throwing up on himself – Ryan Allen was bobbling snaps, and bad punts all game

* Amendola has to catch ball over the middle – that’s how you win championships

* 4th and one lob to Edelman – outsmarted again

* Amazing throw and catch to Gronk down the middle on 4th down

* Thought Brady had Gronk on two point conversion

* Gotta root for NFC now

CONCLUSION

* Because you couldn’t block, kick and extra point or come up with an offense – the country and Goodell won on Sunday

* Last eight games 3-5

* 2-15 on third down

* James White 5 catches on 16 targets

* Look out on the highways tomorrow – 93 and 128 will be a demolition derby

* Butler gave up five receptions on eight targets to Sanders

* Lafell not targeted once

* Chandler Jones not one pressure / just one tackle

* Worst O Line – Cannon, then Vollmer, then Kline, then Fleming

* Brady hit 17 times

* All the ways they lose in playoffs – multiple turnovers, score less than 21, Brady sacked more than twice
