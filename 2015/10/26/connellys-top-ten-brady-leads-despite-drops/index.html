Connelly’s Top Ten: Brady Leads Despite Drops

By Michael in Featured, Now!, Top Story ⋅ October 26, 2015 at 8:17am ⋅ Post a comment

1. Patriots:

MISC

* You know Gisele was getting ready with – “He can’t catch it too!”

* Think Ben Roethlisberger will file a complaint with the league after the Patriots forced TWO penalties by shifting on DEFENSE

* I love Revis seeking out Brady at the end – dude you’re a loser – take your money and screw!

* Another backdoor cover against the Patriots

* How important is a quarterback and coach – Jets have more talent on both lines, backfield, receivers, secondary and lost

* Sideline reporter Evan Washburn was in danger of wedgies throughout the game

* How can the sideline official mis- mark balls by over a yard twice – both having to be overturned

* the bootleg pass is virtually indefensible (as well as the halfback pass)

* What’s the story with Jets getting away with two huge pick plays

* Brady 22-6 versus Jets – yikes

OFFENSE

* Amendola’s third best game as a Patriot (8 for 86 with TD)

* Can Patriots win the Super Bowl if Brady is your leading rusher?

* Another big drop by Edelman (hope not in his head)

* Gronk could have lateralled to Dobson when dragging Jet

* I think we need more Scott Chandler

* No screens again

* James White wouldn’t get yards after catch in a flag football game never mind tackle (secondly, he can’t be caught from behind by a linebacker with end zone in front)

* Why was Lafell still on the field – get back to practice and try another day

* If the Patriots didn’t have ten drops – Revis would have one of his worst days of his career

* Dion Lewis is vital to this team

DEFENSE

* Ninkovich playing above the rim with 4 blocks

* Hightower was flying

* Can they win a Super Bowl with this secondary – Butler is chasing a lot lately

* With all the third down’s Patriots lose on – Mr. Kraft better hope the Fog horn in the lighthouse has a back up generator (Jets 57% on third down)

* Patriots replaced Browner, Arrington and Revis with Butler, Coleman and Ryan

KRAFT SIGHTINGS / BELICHICK GARB

* Bill staying strong with blue windbreaker

* Mr. Kraft 3rd quarter 2:02 and again at 7:16 left in 4th quarter

STATS

* Net punting for Jets = 30.8 yards

* Patriots starting positions: own 32, 39, 41, 46 and Jets 47

* Time of possession 33 to 27 for Jets
