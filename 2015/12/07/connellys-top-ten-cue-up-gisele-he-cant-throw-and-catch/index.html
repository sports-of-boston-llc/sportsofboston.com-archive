Connelly’s Top Ten: Cue up Gisele – He Can’t Throw and Catch

By Michael in Featured, Now!, Top Story ⋅ December 7, 2015 at 9:37am ⋅ Post a comment

1. Patriots – Eagles:

MISC

* Is it me or is it too early in James White’s career to have a goofy dance

* There goes Brady’s MVP

* Hey Siragusa – Tony Larussa called – he wants his glasses back

* They must get the bye

* Tremendous pass by Bradford for that last first down under all kind of pressure (reminded me of the Eli to Tyree pass)

SPECIAL TEAMS

* Blocked punt at the end of the half – why not take a knee at the point – the Eagles weren’t going to call time outs

* Terrible angle by Ebner on the Sproles punt return TD

* Not sure if you needed the second on-side kick

* How many times is Slater going to pick himself up off the ground?

COACHING

* The drop-kick kick off only cost them 20 yards but it was a momentum changer (great play by Eagles #16)

* Bill did a demonstration on holding with the female official

OFFENSE

* Hate that they had to go into their arsenal and give up the pass by Amendola to Brady

* Brady has been hit over 35 times in three short weeks – Vollmer is too big for left tackle

* I know there are no other options BUT – I hate Scott Chandler’s lack of compete / I hate that two years into the program LaFell doesn’t know when to look for the back shoulder pass and keeps breaking off patterns when his QB thinks other / I hate that Keshawn Martin can’t catch at least 50% of the passes that hit his hand (3 catches / 8 targets)

* Thought they could have stayed with Blount a little more early

* Please Josh – don’t run the play action, fake the end around – it has never worked

DEFENSE

* Chandler Jones a December non factor again (no sacks not even a hit a on QB

* Missed Hightower

* Did I see the defense surrendering on the Eagles last Touchdown drive?

* I love Ninkovich’s effort but how many times does he have a free run at the QB and whiff?

* McCourty bowled over by Celek on his TD reception

WORST PLAYS

* Amendola drop running toward end zone

* James White tripping on the five yard line going into the end zone

* Brady forcing a pass into double coverage resulting in a 100 yard return (Canon whiffed on his tackle)

* All the drops – ugh!

* Brady getting frustrated and just heaving the ball

* Lafell not running through the football

BELICHICK GARB / KRAFT SIGHTING

* Blue Hoodie now 1-2

* Kraft early 3:25 left in first quarter with Rupert Murdock being fed by blond (reminded me of Benny Hill skit where young blond wife is trying to kill by serving him bacon and continuously lighting his cigarettes) / 4th quarter with Rupert Murdoch with crazy winter cap working

STATS

* Total Yards – Pats 427 to Eagles 248

* Brady hit 13 times / Bradford 3

* Penalty yards Pats – 30 / Eagles – 97

2. AROUND THE NFL:

* Tom Coughlin going for it on 4th down instead of kicking the field goal is almost a fire-able offense (Giants had a ten minute drive they scored no points on)

* Before Marriota’s 87 yard TD run – Tennessee had been 0-11 at home over last two years

* Every AFC East team won except the Patriots – bet that hasn’t happened in a decade

* Greg Olsen of Carolina having one of the great years ever for a tight end 53 receptions / 788 yards / 6 TD

* How bummed is Peyton Manning right now that the Bronco’s are winning (chicken parm tastes so good)

3. Around College Football:

* If you had an eight team playoff matchups – Clemson – ND / Alabama vs Ohio State / Michigan State vs Iowa / Oklahoma vs Stanford

* Maybe a six team playoff should be next with top two teams getting a bye

* It would be good for College Football to have Navy’s Keenan Reynolds at the Heisman presentation

* Announcers made a big deal about Alabama’s Derrick Henry breaking Hershel’s rushing record – Walker did his in 11 games compared to Henry’s 13

* Riding their horses Saturday – Henry had 44 touches for Alabama / Christian McCaffrey had 44 touches for Stanford

* Why wouldn’t college football spread out their championship games times better – three games at 8:00?

4. Michigan State:

Watching Michigan State’s final 22 play drive (9:04) – it struck me that the Spartan team took on the persona of the coach Mark Dantonio – what other teams take on the image of their coach:

* Georgetown Hoya’s – John Thompson

* UNLV Running Rebels – Tarkanian

* Boston College hockey – Jerry York

* Houston Oilers – Jerry Glanville

* Philadelphia Eagles – Buddy Ryan

* Dallas Cowboys – Tom Landry

* Boston Bruins – Don Cherry

* Denver Nuggets – Doug Moe

5. Patriots Dominance:

In the Patriots last 84 games they have only lost TWO games by more than one score

6. Red Sox Aces:

1971-1978 – Tiant

1984-1996 – Clemens

1998-2004 – Pedro

2006 – 2014 – Lester

2016 – ???? – Price

7. Amazing Stats:

Stanford’s Christian McCaffrey last night against USC vs. Reggie Bush’s legendary night against Fresno State in 2005:

McCaffrey Â – 461 total yards including a touchdown PASS / two TD’s

Bush – 513 total yards including two TD’s

8. Old School:

Speaking of Hershel Walker in his Sophomore and Junior years he carried the ball 720 times in 22 games or 32.7 carries a game

9. Just a Little Bit Outside:

In honor of the heroes of Pearl Harbor – some sad and tragic facts:

* Oil is still leaking – up to 9 quarts a day

* 353 Japanese planes in attack (only 28 shot down)

* Casualties – 2,343 killed / 1,272 wounded / 960 missing

* Six of eight ships sunk were fixed and used during WWII

* Attack only lasted 90 minutes

10. Randomocity:

* Shouldn’t Evgeni Malkin of the Penguins be one of the top 20 players in NHL history with his size and skill set?

* Brad Stevens must have watched Rondo get ejected the other night and be like – thank God I don’t have to deal with him

* Two things on the halftime Dr. Pepper competition – Is it possible to find worst athletes / can they move the apparatus any closer (it’s like a Chad Pennington completion)

* Thank you Charlie, Wally and Victor for letting me steal your material for my Patriots recap – your thoughts sounded a lot more insightful than mine
