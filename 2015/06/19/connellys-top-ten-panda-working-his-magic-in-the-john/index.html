Connelly’s Top Ten: Panda Working His Magic in the John

1. Red Sox:

* You know it’s bad when Atlanta intentionally walks .231 hitting David Ortiz to get to your $20mm cleanup hitter Hanley Ramirez

* How silly do you feel if you have a Red Sox license plate or even worse tattoo?

* Could Cherrington trade Hanley, Panda and Porcello to the Dodgers – we will throw in Nick Punto again

* Panda tweeting during the game – right back to chicken and beer in the clubhouse. No respect for their great fortune.

* Chicago has won more Stanley Cups in six years than the Canadiens have won in 35 years / Bruins in 45 years

* Someone used this barometer on who’s best – if you life depended on it would you want Bird taking the last shot in a playoff game or Lebron (add to better than Lebron – Jordan, Kobe, Kareem, Magic)

* I agree with the discussion on lack of scoring the NHL playoffs – goalie equipment has to be reduced and nets slightly widened

* Duncan Keith is the best skater as a defenseman since Orr (yes better than Coffey)

* Doc Emrick best announcer in sports right now

6. New York as a sports city:

Isn’t New York the most over-rated sports city in the world (other than the Giants):

* Jets haven’t won since 1967

* Mets have won once in 45 years

* Yankees have once in 15 years

* Rangers have won once in 75 years

* Knicks have won twice in 68 years

7. Old School:

Celtics beat Syracuse 111-105 – four overtimes (3/21/53)

* Cousy 30-32 from the free throw line – 50 points

* A fight in the second quarter between Dolph Schayes and Bob Brannum led to their ejections and Syracuse players going after the Boston Police

* Syracuse lost five other players to fouls – In 4th OT, two more Syracuse players fouled out but stayed in the game giving the Celtics the ball after the free throws

* Paul Seymour and Red Rocha of Nationals each played 67 minutes

* 130 total free throws

* Owner Walter Brown had to leave in the second overtime because of stress related head pain / publicist Howie McHugh passed out in the press box

* Globe wrote – “All is not lost because the Braves have left. It gives lie to those who were prophesying the end of Boston as a center of sport”

* Globe wrote – “the greatest one man show ever seen in Boston Garden or any place in this city”

* Syracuse Writer on Cousy: “He’s the greatest thing anybody ever will see any place”
