If we want to acquire Adrian Gonzalez, Prince Fielder, or other, we need a spot to play them.

Ideally I don’t think the Red Sox ever wanted to move a 31 year old Youkilis to 3rd. They have a proven asset at 1st, and why risk that? Come 2011 they probably wont want to move a 32 year old 1st baseman to 3rd.

So if you want to acquire a slugger for this team, the position that will make most sense will be DH.

Unless an Adrian Gonzalez, a slugging 1st baseman with an exceptional glove, comes at a great price, it won’t ever make sense for the team to move Youkilis to 3rd.

Good luck to Ortiz this year.

If it happens to be his last year in a Red Sox uniform, he’s done us proud and left some long LONG lasting memories.
