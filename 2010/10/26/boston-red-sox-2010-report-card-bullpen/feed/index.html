Atchinson is 34 going on 35 while Papelbon is 29 going on 30.

Atchinson had a 4.66 FIP, a .277 BABIP (MLB average .302), and gave up a lot of homeruns (1.35 HR/9). He did have some bad luck other places but overall it appears he’s exactly what he is… a long relief man.

Papelbon on the otherhand: 3.51 FIP, .307 BABIP, .94 HR/9 (MLB average .96). He had a 3.9 ERA but his peripherals say he should have been better.

Flip the grades, Papelbon B, and Atchinson C, and you’re right.
