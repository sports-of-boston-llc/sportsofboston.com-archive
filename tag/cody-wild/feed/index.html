Fri, 02 Jun 2017 11:36:49 +0000 en-US hourly 1 https://wordpress.org/?v=4.5.2 Bruins-Oilers Trade: Marquardt for Wild https://sports-of-boston-llc.github.io/sportsofboston.com-archive/2010/03/03/bruins-oilers-trade-marquardt-for-wild/ https://sportsofboston.com/2010/03/03/bruins-oilers-trade-marquardt-for-wild/#comments Wed, 03 Mar 2010 11:11:13 +0000 http://www.sportsofboston.com/?p=36898 The Bruins made another minor deal on Wednesday night, acquiring minor league defenseman Cody Wild for minor league forward Matt Marquardt. The B’s had originally acquired Marquardt from Columbus for Jonathan Sigalet.

A fifth round pick in the 2006 draft, Wild was a three-year star at Providence College. Playing in the ECHL, Wild tallied 14 assists in 37 games for Springfield.

The Bruins also acquired the rights to defensman Stephen Kampfer earlier in the day from the Ducks.
