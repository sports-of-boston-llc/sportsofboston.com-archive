Last offseason, Tom Thibodeau vacated his role as an assistant on the Celtics bench to take the head coaching job in Chicago, where he led the Bulls to 62 wins and was named the NBA Coach of the Year.

In a similar move, Lawrence Frank is leaving the Celtics’ coaching staff for Detroit, where he was named the Pistons’ head coach last week. Frank only spent a year as an assistant under Doc Rivers and was an adequate replacement for Thibodeau, who was regarded as the mastermind behind Boston’s shutdown defense.
