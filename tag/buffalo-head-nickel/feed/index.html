Hello, and welcome to another exciting installment of “Name That Defense!” I’m everyone’s favorite host, John Scullane, and together we’ll decide on a new name for the Patriots’ five linebacker, six defensive back formation debuted against the Bills Sunday. (Is the name “wildcat” already taken? I like that name.) So without further ado, let’s meet our contestants:

Representing the name Pinsir, because of the ability to attack any offensive movement, is Joey Porter. Representing the name Pagoda Top, because of the layers of defenders, is Drew Brees. Representing the name Buffalo Head Nickel, because of the Nickel formation and because the Pats’ opponent was Buffalo, Bill Belichick. And representing the name Emo Kitty for no reason at all, is Chad Ochocinco.
