The Orioles just ended a 9 game losing streak and starting pitcher Kurt Birkins had to say after the game, “The streak’s over.. Now it’s time to start a new one.”

I hope the Red Sox can help them on their way to another long losing streak.

Clay Buchholz has made one major league start against the Angels where he got the win. He went 6 innings, allowing 3 ER, walking 3 and striking-out 5. He had a gutsy performance. In AAA he had a 3.96 ERA over 38.2 innings and striking-out 55. Today was suppose to be Julian Tavarez start but Wakefield hurt his back and Julian had to step up. Of course he didn’t but it opened up a perfect spot of Clay. He was suppose to start today anyway and now he gets to show himself off in the majors again. He’ll be making his case for being a starter next year.
