Well, fortunately for us rumor mill enthusiasts, Toronto announced they would listen to offers for Roy Halladay. Now while they won’t go out of their way to send out their No. 1 starter, the Jays will make a trade if they get what they’re asking for. So, I’m going to try and determine if any teams will get the deal done this year.

However, the Blue Jays are averse to dealing within the division. Halladay has a full year left on his contract after this season and the Blue Jays don’t want him to be the reason they can’t compete in the division next year.
