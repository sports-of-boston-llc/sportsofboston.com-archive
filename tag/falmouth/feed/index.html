Families were having barbecues and many towns had Independence Day fireworks. Red Sox fans had even more reason to celebrate, after beating the Yankees at Yankee Stadium.

However, according to boston.com, this wasn’t enough to satisfy a group of young people in Falmouth, MA. The group noticed a car stuck in traffic with New York license plates and began to harass the family, accusing them of being Yankees fans.

According to police, the driver stepped out of the car and asked the group to stop since there were children in the car. That’s when 20-year-old Robert D. Correia beat the man with a baseball bat while another member of the group slammed the man’s body into the car.(...)
