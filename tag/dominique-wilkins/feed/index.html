Paul Pierce calmed all those hearts by performing in an spectacular fashion today as he and Lebron James clashed back and forth all game in a similar fashion to Larry Bird and Dominique Wilkins in the 1988 Eastern Conference Semi-finals. Once again, the Celtics player was outscored, but his team was not out-dueled. The Celtics finished off the Cavs today 97-92 to advance on to the Eastern Conference Finals against the Detroit Pistons.
