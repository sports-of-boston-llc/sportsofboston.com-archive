(Courtesy of teamsugar.com)

It’s being reported on Boston.com that Tom Brady and ex-girlfriend Bridget Moynahan have welcomed a baby son into the world.Brady flew to Los Angeles this morning and was not present during Wednesday’s practice. It was widely speculated that his absence was because of the impending birth.

The mother and son are said to be doing well but there’s no further update on Brady or his availability for Friday’s pre-season game against Carolina.

Congrats to Tom and Bridget!
