Then it all came tumbling down. Dustin Predoia may now miss weeks, if not months with a broken foot.

This could not have happened as a worse time. The Red Sox have been on a tear lately, going 7-3 in their last ten games to climb within two games of the AL East Lead and are now a game ahead of the Tampa Bay Rays for the Wild Card. All the success has come on the shoulders of Pedroia.

Over the team’s last ten games, Dustin has 18 hits on 36 at bats, good for A .500 AVERAGE! He also has four home runs, albeit three of them came in one game against the Rockies, and ten RBI. Simply put, at a crucial point in the Sox season, Dustin has been putting up MVP numbers to keep his team alive in the most competitive division in baseball.(...)
