He was signed today (June 16th) and was the first signing of the Patriots’ 2009 NFL draft picks, where the Patriots selected 12 players. The Patriots took Ohrnberger with the 123rd choice overall in the draft. A durable player, coming in at 6’2″ 297 lbs, Ohrnberger started 34 consecutive games in college.
