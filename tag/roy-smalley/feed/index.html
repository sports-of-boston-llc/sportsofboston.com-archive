So, you turned on the Red Sox game Monday and wondered, who is that guy with Don Orsillo?

Well, it’s former Twins shortstop Roy Smalley, who replaced Jerry Remy as the color commentator for NESN’s broadcast of the Red Sox-Twins game.

According to NESN.com, Remy was “under the weather.”

He hopes to be back in the NESN booth for Tuesday’s game.
