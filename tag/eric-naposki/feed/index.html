Naposki and that lover, Nanette McNeal, were arrested by authorities on Wednesday for the murder of William Francis McLaughlin.

Officials said McNeal stood to earn more than $1 million in life insurance from McLaughlin’s death. McNeal was also accused of writing over $100,000 in checks out of McLaughlin’s accounts.

Naposki, who along with his former girlfriend were considered suspects in this case for many years, is reportedly accused of carrying out the murder.
