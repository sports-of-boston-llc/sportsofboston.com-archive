The Boston Celtics have acquired athletic wingman Perry Jones III, a 2019 second round pick from the Detroit Pistons and cash considerations from the Oklahoma City Thunder for a future conditional second round pick.

Jones averaged 4.3 points and 1.8 points per game for the Thunder last season. As Royce Young of ESPN noted, Jones showed people across the league his potential by filling in for superstar Kevin Durant last season. In Durant’s three game absence, Jones displayed his offensive ability by scoring 32, 20, and 16 points respectively.(...)
