Fri, 02 Jun 2017 11:36:49 +0000 en-US hourly 1 https://wordpress.org/?v=4.5.2 Citi Field: My First Impression https://sports-of-boston-llc.github.io/sportsofboston.com-archive/2009/06/08/citi-field-my-first-impression/ https://sportsofboston.com/2009/06/08/citi-field-my-first-impression/#comments Mon, 08 Jun 2009 12:43:26 +0000 http://www.sportsofboston.com/?p=23102 After checking out the new stadium in the Bronx I wanted to check out the new home of the New York Mets. Now, my latest chance to see a game at Citi Field was going to be to wait until the Giants came into town and to buy tickets to see Tim Lincecum make the Mets look foolish. Fortunately, for Mets fans at least, my first trip to Citi Field came much earlier than that as I got to see them in an outing against the Washington Nationals, a perfectly boring team for the Mets to face so that I could wander around the stadium.

For the game, I got to sit in my friend’s seats in left field on the lowest deck. His seats are seven rows from the field and they were actually a great view. I did not mind viewing a game with a similar view to the outfielders. The seats were similar to the ones at the New Yankee Stadium, except they had the ridges of old stadium seats, unlike the seats at the New Yankee Stadium. Also, as with every new stadium, there were cupholders on the backs of the arms of the seats in front.(...)
