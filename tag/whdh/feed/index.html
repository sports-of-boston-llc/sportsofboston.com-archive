Fri, 02 Jun 2017 11:36:49 +0000 en-US hourly 1 https://wordpress.org/?v=4.5.2 Former Miss Florida to Join WHDH Sports Dept https://sportsofboston.com/2008/02/06/former-miss-florida-to-join-whdh-sports-dept/ https://sportsofboston.com/2008/02/06/former-miss-florida-to-join-whdh-sports-dept/#comments Wed, 06 Feb 2008 19:31:05 +0000 http://www.sportsofboston.com/2008/02/06/former-miss-florida-to-join-whdh-sports-dept/ If the Super Bowl hangover is still getting to you, which I’m sure it is, there is some good news. No, it didn’t come from the C’s, who lost last night. It didn’t come from the B’s either, who also lost.

The good news is that former Miss Florida Julie Donaldson will be joining my employer, WHDH-TV 7NBC (Channel 7 locally), to take their opening in the Sports Dept. In all likelihood, Donaldson will be replacing Dave Briggs, who looks to already have another job with Comcast Sports Net.

Donaldson has worked with the Miami Heat and Fox Sports Net in Florida and recently was working in New York covering the Knicks, Yankees, and Mets for SportsNet New York.

You will first see Donaldson covering Red Sox Spring Training in Fort Myers, Florida beginning later this month. Sweet.
