As of right now the Sox at 86-56 have the same amount of wins they had all of last year. They sit 6.5 games ahead of the Yankees.

Dice-K has struggled in his last two games, having allowed more earned runs then innings pithed. Matsuzaka is 8th in baseball in strikeouts and has all the talent in the world. But he can be great or be abysmal. Its hard to know what side will come out.

Leicester sucks. Basically. Look through all his credentials and he is bad. Career in 55.2 innings, he has a 5.50 ERA and 1.51 WHIP. If Dice-K can give the Sox a quality start, the Sox can take the 5th in a row against Baltimore.
