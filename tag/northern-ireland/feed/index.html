Pro sports teams tend to bond during overseas trips. It happened when the new-look Celtics traveled to Rome, Italy in 2007. By June of the next year, they were world champions. Now, I’m not going to make that lofty of a prediction for our beloved Bruins, but they should leave this European trip as a more gelled unit.

The B’s sure seemed gelled during Saturday’s game in Belfast, as they scored five unanswered goals to beat the “Giants” 5-1. They scored three goals in the second to blow it open and help get the win for Tuukka Rask, who finished with 24 saves.
