Last night, it was reported by Sean McAdam that Red Sox first baseman Adrian Gonzalez had been placed on waivers. Earlier today, Josh Beckett was also placed on waivers.

Gonzalez is due just under $5 million for the remainder of this season, and over $20 million per year for the remainder of his contract. Beckett is owed around $4 million for the remainder of this season, and just over $15 million through 2014.

If no teams claim either of the two players (unlikely, given their salaries), the Red Sox would be able to trade both players as if the trade deadline had never passed.(...)
