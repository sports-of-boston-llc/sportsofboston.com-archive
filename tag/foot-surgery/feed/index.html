This is probably expected so soon after foot surgery, but Tom Brady was spotted wearing a walking boot this weekend while in Brazil. The boot is meant to protect that surgically repaired foot.

Brady, who was sporting a new hair style, was Carnival in Brazil with his wife, Gisele Bundchen.

Brady battled the foot injury all season, but still managed to win the NFL MVP Award when he tossed 36 touchdowns against just four interceptions.
