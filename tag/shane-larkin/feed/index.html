Name: Shane Larkin

College: Miami

Position: Point guard

Age: 20

Stats: 6’0″, 170 lbs.

Background

The son of Hall of Fame shortstop Barry Larkin, Shane originally committed to DePaul but transferred to Miami just before the start of his freshman year. By the end of his freshman season, he had earned the starting point guard spot. This year, as a sophomore, he led the Hurricanes with his 14.5 points, 4.6 assists and 2 steals per game, and shot 40.6% from three-point range. He was a second-team All-American, first-team All-ACC, and received the Lute Olson Award as the nation’s best non-freshman.
