A man named Nick proposed to his girlfriend, Melissa, on the big screen at the Garden. The message on the scoreboard read, “Melissa, will you be my Blueshirt bride? Love, Nick.”

Appalled at Nick’s gesture, Melissa covered her mouth in shock. She then shook her head, stood up, grabbed her belongings, and left her seat in a chorus of boos while Nick was left sitting in disbelief.
