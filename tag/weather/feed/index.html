The blizzard that has already been wreaking havoc along the east coast is continuing to do so and will dump nearly two feat in areas at its worst. Already, the NFL’s Sunday schedule has had several changes as a direct result of the snow. Plenty of teams and game times have been affected, as the NFL details:

49ers @ Eagles

Kickoff has been changed from 1:00pm to 4:15pm to give city and stadium workers more time to prepare Lincoln Financial Stadium and the surrounding areas for the game.

Bears @ Ravens

Likewise, Baltimore’s kickoff was moved to 4:15pm, at the behest of Baltimore city officials. The announcement was made Friday, and the Bears’ Friday flight to Baltimore was canceled, leaving them to wait until Saturday.(...)
