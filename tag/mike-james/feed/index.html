Week 7 resulted in a rash of injuries to stars Reggie Wayne, Doug Martin, Jermichael Finley, and Arian Foster.  Wayne is done for the year as is Doug Martin if his labrum is torn as reported. Greg Schiano is still adamant Martin’s not done, so cross your fingers and add Mike James. Foster’s hamstring aggravation appears to be the least serious of the four listed above, but Ben Tate also has a rib injury. Mercifully for Texan fans and Foster owners, Houston is on the bye along with five other teams (Colts, Ravens, Chargers, Bears, Titans).
