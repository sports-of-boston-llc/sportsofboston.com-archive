It’s hard to believe that the NFL season is already a quarter of the way through. With Week 5 approaching, it seemed like a good idea for a top ten poll. The crack team of Patriots writers for Sports of Boston have put together their Top 10 teams along with the biggest surprises (The Bengals are tied for first in the North?) and their biggest disappointments (poor Titans). Take a look and see how they stack up to your Top 10!

Brian Kintz:

  1. New York Giants
  2. Indianapolis Colts
  3. New Orleans Saints
  4. Minnesota Vikings
  5. New England Patriots
  6. Denver Broncos
  7. New York Jets
  8. Baltimore Ravens
  9. Philadelphia Eagles
  10. Cincinnati Bengals

Biggest Surprise: Cincinnati Bengals

While I can’t discredit the hot start by the Broncos, I have to look at the Cincinnati Bengals as my biggest surprise thus far.  Think about it: they are one wild and crazy last second play in Denver from being undefeated.  Carson Palmer has his squad poised to contend in one of the toughest divisions in the league, with all eyes on this week’s match-up with the Ravens, which should prove a true test of Cincy’s staying power in the playoff race.(...)
