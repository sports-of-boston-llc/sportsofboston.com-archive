]]> https://sportsofboston.com/2010/04/30/the-couch-potato-report-celtics-and-bruins-playoffs-kentucky-derby/feed/ 1 Mine That Bird Wins the Kentucky Derby https://sportsofboston.com/2009/05/02/mine-that-bird-wins-the-kentucky-derby/ https://sportsofboston.com/2009/05/02/mine-that-bird-wins-the-kentucky-derby/#comments Sat, 02 May 2009 22:54:45 +0000 http://www.sportsofboston.com/?p=21673 Hours after favorite I Want Revenge was scratched due to injury, 50-1 longshot Mine That Bird, of New Mexico, won the 135th running of the Kentucky Derby on Saturday.

The winner was way behind early in the race, but when the pack hit the stretch, Mine That Bird surged ahead of at least 10 horses to blow away the field at the end.

Jockey Calvin Borel won his second Derby in three years after winning with Street Sense in 2007.

Mine That Bird paid $103.20 for a $2 bet. That’s second only to Donerail, which paid $184.90 in 1913.
