Fri, 02 Jun 2017 11:36:49 +0000 en-US hourly 1 https://wordpress.org/?v=4.5.2 Ex-Sox RP Geremi Gonzalez Killed by Lightning https://sports-of-boston-llc.github.io/sportsofboston.com-archive/2008/05/26/ex-sox-rp-geremi-gonzalez-killed-by-lightning/ https://sportsofboston.com/2008/05/26/ex-sox-rp-geremi-gonzalez-killed-by-lightning/#respond Mon, 26 May 2008 21:46:12 +0000 http://www.sportsofboston.com/?p=2226 Former major league pitcher Geremi Gonzalez (also spelled Jeremi) was killed by a lightning strike in his native Venezuela on Sunday. He was 33. Emergency management official Herman Bracho confirmed Monday that Gonzalez was struck by lightning at a beach.

Gonzalez pitched for five major league teams from 1997-2006, including a stint with the Red Sox in 2005. In that season, Gonzalez was 2-1 in 28 games (3 starts) with a 6.11 ERA and a 1.43 WHIP. His stats were not great, but they are deceiving, as Terry Francona used Gonzalez in a mop-up role when the Sox had a huge lead or were getting blown out. Gonzalez was reliable and always took the ball when asked no matter the situation. Julian Tavarez played a similar role while with the Red Sox up until this season.
