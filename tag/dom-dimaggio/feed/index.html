Fri, 02 Jun 2017 11:36:49 +0000 en-US hourly 1 https://wordpress.org/?v=4.5.2 Dom DiMaggio Dies at 92 https://sportsofboston.com/2009/05/08/dom-dimaggio-dies-at-92/ https://sportsofboston.com/2009/05/08/dom-dimaggio-dies-at-92/#respond Fri, 08 May 2009 10:36:42 +0000 http://www.sportsofboston.com/?p=21945 Former Red Sox great Dom DiMaggio died in his home in Marion, Mass. at around 1 a.m. Friday morning. DiMaggio, the brother of former Yankees legend Joe DiMaggio, was 92.

Dom was a seven-time all-star outfielder with the Red Sox. He hit .298 with a .383 OBP and 87 HRs in 11 seasons in the big leagues, all with the Red Sox. He scored 100 runs six times in his career, and played on the 1946 squad that nearly won the World Series.

He holds the Red Sox record with a 34-game hitting streak in 1948.

Dom DiMaggio is survived by his wife, three children, and six grandchildren.
