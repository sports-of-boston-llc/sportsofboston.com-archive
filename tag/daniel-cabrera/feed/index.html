Mike Lowell singled Manny home to take a 3-2 lead, and the Sox tacked on two more thanks to another Baltimore error to get their five runs.

Boston’s pitiful offense wasted another good start by Josh Beckett, who went six innings, giving up two runs on four hits and three walks with ten strikeouts. Daniel Cabrera was strong as well, going seven innings and allowing just two runs.
