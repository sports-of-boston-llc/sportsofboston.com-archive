ESPN.com’s insider Chad Ford and columnist J.A. Adande discussed the Celtics on Ford’s Daily Dish NBA podcast today. Ford and Adande went in depth first about Los Angeles Lakers star Kobe Bryant, but later discussed how good the Celtics could possibly be this season following the Kevin Garnett trade.

CF – “Do you think that was a good deal for the Timberwolves? Do you agree with their trade?”

JA – “Seems like (T-Wolves GM Kevin McHale) could’ve got a little more out of that. At least, if you’re gonna start over, you might as well go all the way to the bottom. So, now you got young players, you got expiring contracts coming up. I guess you can start fresh. Again, it’s just hard to sell to your fans.”
