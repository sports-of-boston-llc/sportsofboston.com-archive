In two, I am in great position in the top 5. In one, I am in dead last thanks to two factors: Injuries and Lance Berkman hitting under .200. The news did not get any better last week when Cubs’ 3B Aramis Ramirez dislocated his shoulder and will miss at least two months. I just have a pretty good feeling that I won’t be making the championship round like I did last season. Son of a bitch!

Here are some players who didn’t piss off their owners last week.

OFFENSE

Hanley Ramirez (SS), Florida Marlins: My baby boy! Finally living up to his #1 rank, Hanley turned it on (and turned me on) with 16 hits and 11 runs last week. Six of hits were for extra bases (2 doubles, 4 HR) and he had 7 RBI. He even stole three bases to solidify himself as a 30-30 threat again this season.(...)
