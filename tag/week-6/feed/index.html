There was a plethora of news coming out of Foxboro during the Patriots’ (3-1) bye week—beginning with the trading of Randy Moss to the Minnesota Vikings, and ending with the re-acquisition of Deion Branch from the Seattle Seahawks.

There are certainly a lot of question marks for the Pats heading into their Week 6 show down with the Baltimore Ravens (4-1).  A victory over Baltimore, the hottest team in the AFC, will go a long way toward justifying the recent personnel moves made by coach Belichick.

A win might also help the Patriots forget the 33-14 beat down Baltimore laid on them in the playoffs last January.

The Ravens, meanwhile, extended their three-game winning streak in Week 5 with a convincing 31-17 victory over the Denver Broncos.  Many around the league believe the Ravens are the NFL’s best team. With quality wins over the Jets, Steelers, and Broncos, you could certainly make a case for the AFC North juggernaut.
