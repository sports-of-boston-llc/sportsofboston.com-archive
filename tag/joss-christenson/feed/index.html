While Fenway Park staff will be filling trucks with equipment on the streets around the ballpark, inside the park Olympic snowboarders and skiers compete in the Polartec Big Air Fenway on February 11th and 12th.

This one-of a-kind extreme sports event features professional extreme sport athletes. They will compete in a variety of competitions including slopeside snowboarding on a ramp that begins in the outfield, reaches 140 feet above the center field wall and runs Â through the center of the diamond ending at home plate. The highest part of the ramp towers above the center field wall, higher than the park lights. Competitors can be seen from the Massachusetts Turnpike, which runs behind the park’s outfield.
