After a warm Saturday at Fenway Park where Red Sox fans enjoyed a 6-2 victory over the Philadelphia Phillies and witnessed home run number 497 from David Ortiz, the Labor Day Weekend jubilance was quelled by violence. Around 2 a.m. Sunday morning, the time when most of the Fenway bar patrons funnel out onto Landsdowne Street, Yawkey Way, Brookline Ave, and others near by, gunshots were fired on Brookline Avenue. Two women in their 20’s were hit and sustained non-life-threatening injuries. It has been reported by local news stations that one women was shot in the leg and another was grazed on the side, nonetheless both where taking by Boston EMS to area hospitals and are expected to recover.(...)
