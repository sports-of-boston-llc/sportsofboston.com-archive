Wakefield was going for a major league best 17th win until he pulled up with a sore back tonight. Wakefield has posted a decision in every one of his 26 starts this year. He is the 8th pitcher since 1957 to record a decision in his first 26 starts (Source: Dave Smith, Retrosheet). BUT he has been scratched and Julian Tavarez will take the mound tonight to pull the Sox out of their slump.

Tavarez has looked very strong. His last 2 starts against LA Angels and Chicago White Sox he kept to 3 ER in 12 IP. Julian was scheduled to start Saturday but had to be moved up because Wakefield has a sore back

The Orioles are currently on a 9 game losing streak. While the Sox are riding a 3 game losing streak. The last 3 game losing streak the Sox had was July 17th-19th. After that losing streak the Sox went on to win 5 straight.
