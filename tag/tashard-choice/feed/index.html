It’s pretty funny how much everyone overreacts to a couple weeks of football. For example, I’ve been hearing a lot of debate this week about which Steve Smith is the better play for the rest of the season. Is this a serious question? (Note: I understand that with two Steve Smiths this is going to get very confusing…just bear with me.)
