For a Wednesday, it’s been a pretty eventful day at One Patriot Place in Foxboro. Earlier today, it was announced that Tom Brady and wife Gisele Bundchen welcomed a baby boy into the world. People.com reported the story and Brady confirmed at a press conference this morning. Reports are mother and son are doing well, no name has been released.

In other Patriots news, Randy Moss was among four Patriots sent home today for being late to an 8 a.m. meeting. ESPN.com reports Moss, Adalius Thomas, Gary Guyton and Derrick Burgess were late for a team meeting and sent home. The weather in New England is treacherous with snow this morning yet it looks as though everyone else made it on time. Rather predictably, Bill Belichick would not confirm the report.
