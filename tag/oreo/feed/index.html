Tuesday evening at the Prudential Center, Shaquille O’Neal participated in an Oreo Lick Rally. What is that? Good question. But, it may help to know that Shaq signed an endorsement deal with Oreo back in September 2010. He signed on to be part of the Double Stuff Racing League (DSRL) team, along with Eli Manning, Venus Williams, and Apolo Anton Ohno.

Because the footage is so brand new, it hasn’t even hit YouTube yet, but feel free to check out the video on Yahoo! via WBZ-TV Boston.

Had I known about this, I would have been in that contest. Oreos are probably the best cookies ever.
