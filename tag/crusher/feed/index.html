I thought I’d pass along word that the Maine Red Claws mascot, Crusher, has a new doll that is being sold now for a good cause. Proceeds will benefit Maine charities. Not bad!

From the Maine Red Claws Facebook page via Twitter:

Crusher has arrived! Crusher dolls are now available. All proceeds from the $8 doll will benefit Maine charities through the Maine Red Claws Foundation. Limited quantities. Order yours today by calling 207-210-6655 or stop by our offices at 413 Congress St, Portland.
