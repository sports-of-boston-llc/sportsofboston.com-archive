Doron Lamb: 20 years old, 6’5″; 199 lbs

School: Kentucky

Position: Shooting Guard

Background:

Doron Lamb is one of the rare players who actually played for more than one season under John Calipari at Kentucky. After winning a national championship and being named to the All-SEC second team, Lamb decided to enter the NBA draft. Lamb is a versatile scorer with a deadly jump shot. This past season Lamb averaged 13.7 points and shot 47% field from the field.

He is a big time scorer with a smooth game. Lamb doesn’t have the greatest size, but makes up for it with hustle and agility.(...)
