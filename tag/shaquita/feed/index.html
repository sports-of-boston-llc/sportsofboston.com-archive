Shaq, or should I say “Blackie Bulger,” recently posed as a Shaqtue. Why? We may never know. All we care to know is what’s next from the Big Shamrock?

Shaq is making Halloween plans, and he wants everyone to know, according to WEEI. At first, he wasn’t going to do another public outing until the winter.

“I thinking of buy a snowsuit, standing in the middle of a field and have people throw snowballs at me,” he said. “Something different.”

That would be interesting, and it still may happen, but Shaq’s next plans are on Sunday. He told the radio station he has never taken the T in Boston, but he will do it on Sunday, which also happens to be Halloween. He reportedly plans on cross-dressing as his female alter-ego “Shaquita.”(...)
