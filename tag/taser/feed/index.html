In case you haven’t heard, the stepfather of Marquis Daniels was arrested and Tasered during Game 2 of the Celtics-Magic series on Wednesday night. Yes, Tasered…because he was resisting arrest.

The altercation began when the man, Willie L. Buie, was taunted and hollered at by Magic fans. The argument spilled into the stands, where Buie was arrested and brought out of the arena. In a hallway away from the court, Buie was Tasered while resisting arrest, and it was all caught on cell phone video.
