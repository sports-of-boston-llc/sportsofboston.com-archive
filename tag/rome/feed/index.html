The Celtics are now 1-0 in the preseason, while the loss brings the Raptors’ record to 0-1.

Pierce and new teammates Garnett, Ray Allen, and Eddie House all scored in double figures. PG Rajon Rondo added 6 assists in 25 minutes and center Kendrick Perkins grabbed 7 rebounds in 23 minutes. (Full box score from NBA.com)
