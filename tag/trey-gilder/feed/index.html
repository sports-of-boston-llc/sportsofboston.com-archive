Charlotte’s Ajinca Assigned To NBA D-League’s Maine Red Claws

Charlotte Bobcats center Alexis Ajinca was assigned to the Maine Red Claws, the Bobcats’ NBA Development League affiliate. The assignment is the fifth of the NBA D-League season, the second for Maine, and the second for Ajinca, who was assigned to the Sioux Falls Skyforce last season. Scoring in double-figures in eight of 11 games in the NBA D-League last season, Ajinca averaged 11.9 points, 6.8 rebounds and 22.8 minutes.

Red Claws Acquire Trey Gilder

Trey Gilder has signed an NBA Development League contract and joined the Maine Red Claws. Gilder was a member of the Memphis Grizzlies’ undefeated 2009 Vegas Summer League roster. He posted 5.4 points and 2.8 rebounds on 63.2 percent shooting in 12.8 minutes in five games and signed as a free agent with the Grizzlies on August 27, 2009. A key reserve for the 2009 NBA D-League Champion Colorado 14ers of the NBA D-League last season, Gilder averaged 13.7 points and 5.5 rebounds in 23.9 minutes in 50 games as the team posted the D-League’s best regular season record (34-16) and captured the NBA D-League title. He averaged 10.5 points and 3.5 rebounds in 14.8 minutes per game in all four of the team’s postseason contests.(...)
