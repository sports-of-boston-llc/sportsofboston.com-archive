Celtics center Shaquille O’Neal (or should we call him “Blackie Bulger“?) has apparently applied for his gun license in Sudbury, according to a Channel 5 report. Shaq stopped by the Sudbury Police Department to pick up his permit, but officers wouldn’t confirm that fact.

“Shaq stopped by the police station sometime last week. It was not related to any police work. He just wanted to introduce himself,” said Lt. Scott Nix.

Shaq has served in law enforcement in Miami, Virgina, and Cleveland, so this could be a firs step in him wanting to join a local police force in the area.(...)
