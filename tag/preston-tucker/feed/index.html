Boston has been bruised, battered, and beaten in every facet of the game on their tough 7-game swing through Los Angeles and Houston. The Sox dropped four straight to the Angels in a sweep, something that has never happened on the Angels turf in the 55-year history of the series between the two teams. In four games the Sox were outscored 22-4 by the red hot LA club who beat up on Sox with the long ball and great starting pitching. The song remained the same in Houston where they were again outscored 17-9 and swept in a 3-game set. (...)
