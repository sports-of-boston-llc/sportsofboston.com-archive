He managed the Red Sox from 1981 up until he retired in 1984. Over his 20 years as a major league manager, Houk won 1,619 times over 3,157 games, finishing with a career winning percentage of .514.

He managed a young rookie pitcher named Roger Clemens in his final season. That year, Clemens was 9-4 with a 4.32 ERA at the age of 21.
