Fri, 02 Jun 2017 11:36:49 +0000 en-US hourly 1 https://wordpress.org/?v=4.5.2 Garnett Injured in Loss to Jazz https://sports-of-boston-llc.github.io/sportsofboston.com-archive/2009/02/20/garnett-injured-in-loss-to-jazz/ https://sportsofboston.com/2009/02/20/garnett-injured-in-loss-to-jazz/#respond Fri, 20 Feb 2009 13:03:44 +0000 http://www.sportsofboston.com/?p=16531 The Celtics suffered two rare losses Thursday night against the Jazz. They held a 66-60 lead after the third quarter and by as much as 11. Then, Utah outscored Boston 30-19 in the fourth while shooting 12-17 from the field to earn the comeback win, 90-85. How did the Celtics manage to blow the lead?

Well, they played the entire second half without their best defender, Kevin Garnett, who left the game with a right knee strain after a botched alley-oop. Garnett warmed up before the second half, but was held out as a precaution. He said after the game that his knee will be re-examined Friday.(...)
