The Future Looks Bright

Coming off of an unexpected playoff appearance, the Celtics look like a team on the rise in the Eastern Conference. Danny Ainge has put together a promising mix of young prospects and proven NBA players that seems to thrive under the tutelage of Brad Stevens. This group of guys has bought in to Stevens’ system and seem to take pride in getting stops on the defensive end. This is an encouraging development for Celtics fans who’ve had legitimate concerns about the team since the end of the Big Three era, and the Rondo trade should have finally consummated these tensions about the direction of the team. Ainge seems to have settled on his core group and now needs to build around those guys. With free agency looming, it’s time to start thinking about potential targets.(...)
