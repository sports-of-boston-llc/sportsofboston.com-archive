A report from the Oregonian tells the story of current NBA free agents’ pursuit for a job. Some, like former Bucks forward Ruben Patterson are nervous they remain unsigned at this juncture.

“You could say that. I know it’s the middle of August. You got a lot of guys who haven’t signed,” Patterson said last week during a telephone interview from Cincinnati.

Probably in hopes of attracting some interest from teams, Patterson blatantly told the Oregonian’s Geoffrey C. Arnold that he wanted to play for the Celtics or the Heat because they could use an “energy guy.”
