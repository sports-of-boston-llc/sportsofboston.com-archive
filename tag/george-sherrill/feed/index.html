]]> https://sportsofboston.com/2008/07/18/tgif-first-half-awards/feed/ 1 AL East Fantasy Preview: Baltimore Orioles https://sportsofboston.com/2008/02/19/al-east-fantasy-preview-baltimore-orioles/ https://sportsofboston.com/2008/02/19/al-east-fantasy-preview-baltimore-orioles/#comments Wed, 20 Feb 2008 03:56:50 +0000 http://www.sportsofboston.com/2008/02/19/al-east-fantasy-preview-baltimore-orioles/ Baltimore has taken a bold step in the direction of the franchise and have decided to rebuild their team. Baltimore traded two of its biggest names in SS Miguel Tejada and ace pitcher Erik Bedard. Bedard stayed in the AL by being traded to Seattle and Tejada made the jump to the NL with the Houston Astros. Both players have left big holes at their positions and also they left a lot of questions and uncertainties. You may be asking yourself who on this team will have a significant impact on your fantasy season? Well let us have a look:

OFFENSE

Nick Markakis, OF: Markakis followed up his impressive rookie year with a fantastic sophomore season. He had 23 HR and 112 RBI for an Oriole club that was short on offense. Heck, he even threw in 18 steals. He is still young and developing so the skills he has now are only getting better. However, with Baltimore rebuilding, I can’t expect Markakis to put up similar RBI and run numbers. He may be the only source of power and he also has the stolen base potential.

Pete’s Prediction: 90 R, .290 AVG, 28 HR, 96 RBI, 16 SB
