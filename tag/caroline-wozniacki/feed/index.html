With temperatures poking into the 90s, this year’s US Open is already becoming difficult to handle. That’s even more so for Denmark’s Caroline Wozniacki, who during her second round match with Aliaksandra Sasnovich of Belarus got her hair tangled up in her racket. Still, that didn’t stop her from making an attempt at a shot, and moving pretty handily into the third round.

First bees, now this? But in the strange world of sports, this could finally be the good luck charm that wins Wozniacki, formerly ranked #1 in the world, her first major.
