Pablo Sandoval is the talk of Red Sox Spring Training for arriving to camp overweight. The media that follow the Boston team is abuzz with the news. They are most focused on Sandoval’s inability to control himself after San Francisco Giants color analyst, Mike Krukow, reported to local, California sports talk station KNBR that the Giants franchise knew about Sandoval’s eating habits.

I do not blame Sandoval. I do blame Ben Cherrington for not doing an appropriate level of due diligence on the third basemen when they plucked him away from the San Francisco Giants. Yes, he moved well on the field and at the plate in 2012 – 2013. I cannot help but speculate that the Giants knew Pablo’s star was no longer rising. (...)
