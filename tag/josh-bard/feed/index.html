Bard, 30, was vying for a role behind Jason Varitek but didn’t impress enough in Spring training.

Bard has played seven season in MLB with the Indians, Red Sox and Padres and has a .265 career average.

My Take: Some people had high hopes that Bard would be a better option than Varitek given the captain’s offensive struggles, but I never saw a switch happening. The Sox are still on the lookout for a young catch who could step in as the next franchise back stop.
