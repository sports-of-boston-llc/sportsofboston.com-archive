In case you didn’t know, Delonte West and Kanye West are two different people. But apparently that didn’t stop Delonte, the guard from the Boston Celtics, from deciding to drop a mix tape. I can’t say he’s completely terrible–After all, anyone who can freestyle about Kentucky Fried Chicken for about six minutes has to have some talent.

But then again, this is certainly no Wu-Tang. The lyrics in his preview are a bit too generic for my taste (What exactly is “about to go down?”) and the beats aren’t particularly special either. I’d tell Delonte to stick to basketball, but who knows when the next NBA game will be due to the lockout.  Practice makes perfect, so this can only go uphill.  But in everyone’s best interest, let’s just hope the lockout ends sooner rather than later.(...)
