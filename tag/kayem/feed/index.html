How about Fenway Franks?

Well, fans now have their chance to create Fenway’s next frank. Kayem Foods is launching the first-ever “Create The Next Fenway Frank” contest, which gives fans the opportunity to offer their own favorite combination of hot dog toppings for enjoying a game at Fenway Park.

The winning hot dog will be announced on July 23, which is National Hot Dog Day. The contest, though, runs through June 15. For more information, visit facebook.com/kayemfranks.

On July 1, Kayem will reveal the top five hot dogs submitted by fans. The finalists will be brought to Boston to help rally support for their franks on July 23. At the event that day, a Red Sox player will announce the grand prize winner.

The winning recipe will be unveiled as part of a ceremony prior to the Red Sox-Rays game that night at Fenway, and the winner’s dog will be sold at Fenway. The winner also receives a $1,000 cash gift card.

A runner-up will receive a $500 cash gift card.
