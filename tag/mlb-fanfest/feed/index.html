Thanks to my mother’s friend, I procured two free tickets to a night session for people who received tickets exclusively from Bank of America. It was held at the Jakob Javits Center on the West Side of Manhattan, a place used for many conventions. Besides food, everything was free, which was pretty awesome. I attended with my brother and for the most part we had a great time, but wished that we only had more time to spend at the Fest.
