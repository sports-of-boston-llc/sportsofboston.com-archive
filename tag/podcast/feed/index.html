Today marks the debut of the new Sports Of Boston podcast (I know, I’ve re-named it three times now), called Point Taken. The new show features segments, weekly guest, and co-host — Brian Moller. We’ll have a brand new episode every Monday, Wednesday, and Friday.

Part 1

We talk Haynesworth, Kyle Arrington’s Twitter, Ochocinco’s Struggles, The Bruins Resurgence, and the Erin Andrews/ESPN controversy..

Click Play to Listen! Using a cell phone? Click Here!

Part 2

We talk with WEEI’s Michael Holley about his new book War Room , working at the Boston Globe, Joe Paterno, the radio wars & much more!

Click Play Below To listen! Using a Cell Phone? Click Here!

Below is a breakdown of the conversation for those who wish to skip around…

0:00 – 6:00 – Breaking down the “Unguarded” doc

6:00 – 16:00 – Talking about the premise of War Room and also the subordinates that have come & gone around The Hoodie. (And, gasp, some Spy-Gate references)

16:00 – 21:30 – The state of the Patriots now. Is the dynasty over?

21:30 – 30:30 – Why Michael left the Globe & some fun stories from his days in the newspaper industry..

30:30 – 39:00 – The Penn State scandal.

39:00 – END – Diving into the local media scene: The Sports Lodge, WEEI vs 98.5, and much more!
