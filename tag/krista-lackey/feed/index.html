Like him or not, John Lackey has been really bad this season. How bad? He’s sporting an ERA of 6.41 over 160.0 innings, the worst ERA in the bigs this season by more than .5 runs (min. 100 innings).

Could we now have a reason for his horrendous season? Earlier this season, we found out about John Lackey’s wife Krista is battling breast cancer. Then, Monday morning, TMZ.com reported that Lackey filed for divorce from his wife.

Lackey and Krista, who had a prenup, have been married since 2008 and do not have a child together. According to the court documents filed in Texas, Lackey said “the marriage has become insupportable because of discord or conflict of personalities.” (...)
