In a cute production put together by MLB.com, David Ortiz spent his off-day on Thursday at the MLB Fan Cave in New York City. In the video (see below), the Sox slugger complained about being so hated in New York and said he wanted to change that.

He then hit the streets of New York while wearing his Red Sox jersey and sought hugs from random fans walking (and sometimes driving) around. Some people obliged, while others resisted the hug.
