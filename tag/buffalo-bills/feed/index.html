We all know every single year the Patriots win and pretty much dominate the AFC East.  As I don’t see this changing anytime soon, I do expect the other 3 teams to have a competitive season to see who comes in second in the east.  The Jets, Bills and Dolphins are all very similar to each other.  Not similar with personnel or coaching, but similar in the way that all could end up with close to the same records.

Miami Dolphins

The Dolphins who finished 6-10 last year seem to always have potential to challenge, or make the Pats look over their shoulder.  However they can never fulfill this potential.  With Adam Gase as the head coach, a lot of Dolphins fans are excited.  Ryan Tannehill is young and should keep improving, their defense can be really good, and they have one of the best play makers in the NFL in Jarvis Landry.  The Dolphins could be a surprise in the East. With that said I have them at 7-9.

Buffalo Bills

Buffalo finished with a 8-8 record last season.  However, they were really in the hunt all season.  Tyrod Taylor should improve, and as much as I hate to say it Rex Ryan is a good defensive head coach.  They really need a good season from their receivers for a chance to contend. I have the Bills at a solid 9-7.

New York Jets

Yes the hated Jets.  They posted a 10-6 record last season, and had a chance in the final game of the season to secure a playoff spot.  Yet they lost the game in typical Jets fashion, they do have momentum coming into this season.  They have a win now kind of roster, and need to pony up the money and pay Fitzpatrick.  Todd Bowles is a good head coach and could steer the Jets into a wild card spot this upcoming season.  Jets will have another 10-6 season.

All three of these teams could have good seasons for their standards.  None of them will be able to really compete with The Patriots at the end of the day.  Even if Brady is suspended.  Patriots go 13-3 and will get the number 1 seed in the AFC.
