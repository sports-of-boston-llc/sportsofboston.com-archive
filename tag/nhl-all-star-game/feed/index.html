Bruins goaltender Tim Thomas earned his third straight All-Star Game win, stopping 11 of 15 shots in the third period to earn the 11-10 win for Team Lidstrom. Zdeno Chara, who was playing for Team Staal, finished with two assists.

]]> https://sportsofboston.com/2008/11/05/nhl-all-star-ballots-released-tim-thomas-snubbed/feed/ 5 Tim Thomas Added to NHL All-Star Roster https://sports-of-boston-llc.github.io/sportsofboston.com-archive/2008/01/22/tim-thomas-added-to-nhl-all-star-roster/ https://sportsofboston.com/2008/01/22/tim-thomas-added-to-nhl-all-star-roster/#respond Tue, 22 Jan 2008 23:57:40 +0000 http://www.sportsofboston.com/2008/01/22/tim-thomas-added-to-nhl-all-star-roster/ Bruins goalie Tim Thomas was added to the Eastern Conference’s All-Star roster for the NHL All-Star game to be played this coming Sunday, January 27. He’s a replacement for Devils goalie Martin Brodeur.

Thomas has been stellar for the B’s this season, leading the league in save percentage (.930). The Bruins netminder is also eighth in the NHL in GAA (2.30).

Thomas quote obtained from the Globe’s Fluto Shinzawa:

“It feels great. I was really disappointed when I didn’t get named. Now that I have been named, I’m excited.”
