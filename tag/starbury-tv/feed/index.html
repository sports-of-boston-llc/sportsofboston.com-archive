NBA point guard Stephon Marbury has a 24-hour live video chat on the Web site, Justin.tv. On his channel, “Starbury TV,” viewers can see him dancing, eating Vaseline, and offering words of wisdom to the public. Those who view these videos are incredibly perplexed by his ludicrous actions and must be wondering if he is crazy or just desperately trying to get signed. In this case, one has definitely flown over the cuckoo’s nest.

In his dance video, he is one of the many to give a subtle tribute to Michael Jackson, dancing shirtless to MJ while wearing a facial mask in his bathroom. While this may not be as frightening as Shaq in the film Kazaam, his following videos caused some to wonder if he is on a drug binge or suffering from mental problems.(...)
