There wasn’t one big hit by the Baltimore Orioles Tuesday night that the Boston Red Sox could blame for their 4-1 loss to the Orioles at Camden Yards. No, it was all the little hits that did Boston in.

Twelve, to be exact.

Clay Buchholz gave up four runs on 12 hits – only two for extra bases – and two walks in 6.2 innings Tuesday. The Orioles, meanwhile, got excellent pitching from starter Zach Britton, reliever Jim Johnson and closer Kevin Gregg, who combined to hold the Red Sox to just one earned run on six hits and two walks.(...)
