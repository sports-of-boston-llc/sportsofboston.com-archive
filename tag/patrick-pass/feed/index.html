Fri, 02 Jun 2017 11:36:49 +0000 en-US hourly 1 https://wordpress.org/?v=4.5.2 Patriots Sign Patrick Pass https://sports-of-boston-llc.github.io/sportsofboston.com-archive/2009/06/04/patriots-sign-patrick-pass/ https://sportsofboston.com/2009/06/04/patriots-sign-patrick-pass/#comments Fri, 05 Jun 2009 01:14:15 +0000 http://www.sportsofboston.com/?p=23007 The Pats added to an already deep backfield today with the signing of Patrick Pass. Pass played for the Pats from 2000-2006 and won three Super Bowls. He has amassed 1,096 career yards from scrimmage and four TDs.

My Take: It’s tough to say what kind of impact this will have. With so many options at running back, it may be tough to get Pass some touches. He may be used on special teams and as a blocker.
