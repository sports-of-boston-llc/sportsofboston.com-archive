KG, Shaq & co. appeared on Fox’s “The Cleveland Show” on Sunday, and were thankfully as funny as they are talented on the court. During game moments, LeBron James even got an assist on Shaq’s bucket. And between KG, James, and Shaq all deciding not to dribble anymore, Dwight Howard getting emotional, Dirk Nowitzki getting used to English insults, Dwyane Wade leading him the wrong way and Steve Nash’s laughing fits, it was a quality show. Everyone even provided their own voices, of course. Too bad Garnett’s mom didn’t do the same, though.

Maybe Shaq’s joke about hoping the refs “don’t call him for travelling” before leaving for Virginia was a little corny, but when protagonist Cleveland Brown made up with the All-Stars at the end and LeBron James shed a tear, Cleveland’s stepson Rallo did remark that “LeBron James does care about Cleveland.”
