Fri, 02 Jun 2017 11:36:49 +0000 en-US hourly 1 https://wordpress.org/?v=4.5.2 Red Sox Send Down Tazawa, Call Up McBeth https://sportsofboston.com/2009/08/29/red-sox-send-down-tazawa-call-up-mcbeth/ https://sportsofboston.com/2009/08/29/red-sox-send-down-tazawa-call-up-mcbeth/#comments Sat, 29 Aug 2009 23:49:06 +0000 http://www.sportsofboston.com/?p=27432 The Red Sox sent down starter Junichi Tazawa Saturday, and called up reliever Marcus McBeth, who last pitched in the major leagues in 2007 with the Reds. Tazawa was 2-3 with a 6.65 ERA in five games (four starts), and will probably be back in September. For now, he was sent to the Rookie-Level Gulf Coast League Red Sox.

McBeth was called up earlier this season from July 31 to August 1, but did not get into a game.
