You can chalk this up to a slow Wednesday, or perhaps throw it in the Tom Brady man crush category, but whatever the case may be the Patriots quarterback has done away with his “floppy” flow.

At Sports of Boston we have done our best to keep up with Brady’s ever-changing air styles.

  • In October 2010, despite Bill Belichick’s dispute that this never happened, there was a report surfacing that Randy Moss and Brady actually got in a fight over Brady’s hair.
  • In March 2011, the ponytail was born. (We’re still trying to figure out why)
  • In May 2011, there were the odd pictures of Brady (and his flow) going down a water slide.

Now, Brady sports a more professional, shorter look. In the past Brady and the Patriots have had success when No. 12 has a shorter look, so we’ll see if this pays off moving forward.

As of right now, Brady is undefeated with the haircut and has thrown 0 interceptions. He’ll look to keep it that way Sunday afternoon against the Oakland Raiders.
