He was the third base coach in Milwaukee before his promotion.

The Brewers had just lost four straight to the Philadelphia Phillies, leading to a tie atop the Wild Card standings between the Phils and Brew Crew at 83-67. A recent hot streak by the Astros has them currently just 2.5 games back in the Wild Card race.
