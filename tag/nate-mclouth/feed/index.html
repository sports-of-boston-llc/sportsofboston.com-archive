As it stands as of this writing, the Braves are 11th in the N.L. with 4.38 runs scored per game, 12th in HR, and are hitting .258 as a team. The Pirates were almost neck and neck with Braves for offensive futility by being 12th in runs scored (4.33), 10th in HR, and hitting .264 as a team.

When McLouth makes his debut for the Braves, he will already be the team leader in HR, RBI, and SB.
