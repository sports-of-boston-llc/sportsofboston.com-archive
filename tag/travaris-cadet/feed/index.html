There’s a reason football games are 60 minutes and Sunday’s game used up every single one. It was a game where the Patriots looked in firm control of after a strong first half, but almost gave it away with cautious play-calling in the second half. The Patriots did everything they could to lose this game and yet they still came away with the dramatic win over the previously undefeated Saints.

– After a miserable offensive performance last week against the Bengals it was refreshing to see the Patriots put together a nice no huddle drive to start the game. They rattled off 14 plays in just over 5 minutes but the drive stalled when Tom Brady was sacked on 3rd and 7. They got a field goal out of the drive, but it was a bit of a let down considering how well they marched down the field.Â (...)
