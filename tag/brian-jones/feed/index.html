With tight ends Ben Watson and Kyle Brady both nursing injuries, New England needed to bolster its tight end corps for the pre-season. The 6’3″, 258 lb Allen is the fifth tight end in camp now and is perceived as a long shot to make the final roster. He has nine career catches for 146 yards and a touchdown.

The Pats held a light walk through practice Sunday at Gillette without pads. A few noticeable attendees were injured wideout Randy Moss, and tight ends Watson and Brady. The only non-injured player absent was cornerback Asante Samuel. No other word has been leaked as to the status of contract negotiations between the Patriots and Samuel.
