The rain bothered San Diego Padre and Boston Red Sox players alike Wednesday afternoon in Fenway, but no one was affected more than John Lackey.

Lackey’s lack of control allowed the Padres to score four times in the fourth inning, and the game was called in the middle of the eighth and the Padres beat Boston 5-1. It was Boston’s first series loss since getting swept at home by the Chicago White Sox three weeks ago.(...)
