Do you remember the NFL Europe? How about Rohan Davey? If you answered ‘yes’ to the first question, then chances are that you remember the 6’2″, 245-pound quarterback that was hailed the “Golden Arm.” On the contrary, if you don’t have a clue to what the NFL Europe was, then you probably never heard of Rohan Davey.

Davey, who is now 32 years old, attended Louisiana State University, where he threw for 3,347 yards and 18 touchdowns during his senior season. Scouts drooled over Davey’s big-time arm and presence in the pocket, but the general consensus was that Davey lacked NFL accuracy. Still, the Patriots — apart of the Bill Belichick and Nick Saban pipeline — drafted Davey in the fourth round of the 2002 NFL Draft.(...)
