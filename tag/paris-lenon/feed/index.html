Fri, 02 Jun 2017 11:36:49 +0000 en-US hourly 1 https://wordpress.org/?v=4.5.2 Patriots Sign Paris Lenon https://sports-of-boston-llc.github.io/sportsofboston.com-archive/2009/05/28/patriots-sign-paris-lenon/ https://sportsofboston.com/2009/05/28/patriots-sign-paris-lenon/#respond Thu, 28 May 2009 09:01:14 +0000 http://www.sportsofboston.com/?p=22755 The Patriots signed former Detroit Lions linebacker Paris Lenon on Wednesday to help a depleted linebacker corps. The Patriots needed some linebacker help after missing out on Jason Taylor and Ray Lewis, and the injury to third round pick Tyrone McKenzie.

Lenon is a veteran of seven NFL seasons, four with Green Bay and three with Detroit in which he played in all 112 games, starting the last 63. The Lions finished 0-16 in 2008, so the Lenon will be looking to win his first NFL game since 2007 if he stays on with the Pats.
