Has anyone copyrighted “Fantasy Pro Wrestling”? Seriously, that would be a game that could take off instantly. You draft professional wrestlers and put them in your lineups and keep score based on their win/loss records. The only problem I see with it is the fact that there is no end to a pro wrestling “season”. It goes 365 days a year. Once that part of the plan is worked out, I think a million dollar idea will be born.
