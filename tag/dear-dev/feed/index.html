  • Do you love Fantasy?
  • Do you hate Fantasy because you suck at it? (Really, it’s ok to just admit it, it’s the first step to recovery)
  • Is Fantasy more confusing than your not-so secret obsession with the Jonas Brothers?

    • If you answered yes to any of those questions then you need (help if you said yes to the last one and) ‘Dear Dev’.

    ‘Dear Dev’ will be a weekly column made for the people who love Fantasy sports the most… YOU!

    Who decides what is covered? Well, you do silly.

    That’s right folks, you just email lil old me your burning Fantasy (Baseball OR Football) questions and I will do my best to not totally insult your intelligence answer them. You don’t have to keep the emails clean (I always love a good laugh), but I will have to edit them if they are to be posted, gotta watch out for the kiddies.

    So what are you waiting for? If you aren’t in 1st then you’ve got questions and even if you are in 1st, you should have questions too. As anyone who has done Fantasy leagues knows, 1st only matters when it’s all said and done.

    Send all inquiries to dev AT sportsofboston.com with Dear Dev in the Subject line.

    Please don’t post your questions as comments!
