After 15 seasons and a school record 247 wins, Wolff was dismissed as head coach in March following another first round exit in the America East tournament. After a nation-wide search of qualified candidates, BU Athletic Director Mike Lynch decided on Villanova assistant Pat Chambers to take the Terriers to the next level. Coach Chambers took some time out of his busy schedule to talk to Sports of Boston.
