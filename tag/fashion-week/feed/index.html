Boston Celtics’ star point guard, Rajon Rondo, has added to his busy offseason this week by taking an internship with GQ magazine during New York City’s Fashion Week. Rondo should be able to fit the internship in since NBA training camps don’t open until September 28th.

GQ announced the presence of Rondo at their Fashion Week headquarters in a press release this morning and reported that he would be working closely with the magazine’s creative director, Jim Moore, and his fashion department staff.(...)
