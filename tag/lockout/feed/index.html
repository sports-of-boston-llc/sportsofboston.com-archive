On Saturday, August 13, 2011 at 2:05 AM Beaumont Police officers noticed a large crowd outside a club called “The Ticket”, at 3684 College.  Many of the people leaving the club were holding their faces and complaining of fights and pepper spray inside the club.  When officers went inside they observed the staff of the club trying to get everyone to leave.  According to the arresting officer, he observed a subject later identified as Kendrick Perkins having a disturbance with the management of the club.(...)
