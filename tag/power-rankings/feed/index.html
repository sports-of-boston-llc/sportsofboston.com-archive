1. San Diego Chargers

7. Philadelphia Eagles
8. New England Patriots
9. Green Bay Packers
10. Baltimore Ravens

Super Bowl Winner: Minnesota Vikings

This team has everything you need to win a Super Bowl: a strong running game, a quarterback who handles big games, and a defense that can shut an opponent down. They get a week break, which might be what Brett Favre needs. Should they make the big game, expect the Vikings to finally win their first Super Bowl.

Super Bowl Loser: San Diego Chargers

They have the same formula as the Vikings, but who knows how Philip Rivers will handle himself in a Super Bowl. Still, they are the best team in the AFC and even if they have to go to Indianapolis, they should roll through the Colts. The only obstacle they could face? The Jets. Seriously.
