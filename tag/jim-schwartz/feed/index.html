Two of the top teams in the NFL, the Lions and 49ers, locked horns on Sunday, with San Francisco prevailing 25-19. As the final horn sounded, Niners coach Jim Harbaugh ran on the field pumping his fist and flailing his arms in celebration.

As Harbaugh ran out to shake hands with Lions coach Jim Schwartz, he gave him a hellish handshake and pat on the back, and then proceeded to gloat about his win some more.

Schwartz took offense, and ran after Harbaugh. The two then proceeded to shout and had to be separated by players and officials.(...)
