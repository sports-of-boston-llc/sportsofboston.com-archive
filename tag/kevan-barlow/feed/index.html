Fri, 02 Jun 2017 11:36:49 +0000 en-US hourly 1 https://wordpress.org/?v=4.5.2 Morris Placed on Injured Reserve https://sports-of-boston-llc.github.io/sportsofboston.com-archive/2007/11/02/morris-place-on-injured-reserve/ https://sportsofboston.com/2007/11/02/morris-place-on-injured-reserve/#respond Fri, 02 Nov 2007 20:37:29 +0000 http://www.sportsofboston.com/2007/11/02/morris-place-on-injured-reserve/ Backup running back Sammy Morris has been placed on injured reserve, ending his season.

Morris, 30, injured his chest against the Cowboys and hasn’t seen the field since. The true extent of his injury hasn’t been released but Morris hasn’t sniffed the practice field in weeks.

My Take: This is a pretty big loss for the Pats. Morris was outstanding when Laurence Maroney was out with a groin injury and is the tough inside runner the Patriots now lack near the goal line. With Maroney and Kevin Faulk now the primary running backs, look for the Pats to sign someone else. They worked out Kevan Barlow a few weeks ago and I think he would be a great fit in a Patriots uniform.

ESPN.com reports that a source told the Boston Herald that Morris has injured “the bone connecting the sternum to the clavicle.”

With Laurence Maroney’s status up in the air and an ailing backfield ahead of Sunday’s game against Miami, the Patriots worked out Kevan Barlow yesterday at Gillette Stadium.

Head coach Bill Belichick has not commented on Morris’ injury.
