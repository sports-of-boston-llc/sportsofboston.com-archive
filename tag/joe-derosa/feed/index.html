The fan threw the ball right back, and DeRosa ordered that security remove the fan from his seat.

According to The Orlando Sentinel, the fan is Wyndham Vacation Ownership CEO Franz Hanning, who was relocated to another seat. The Sentinel also said Hanning is an acquaintance of Boston Celtics coach Doc Rivers, who lives in Orlando.

The NBA is reportedly reviewing the incident. What should they decide? Should DeRosa be fined/suspended? What about Hanning? Check out the video to decide for yourself.
