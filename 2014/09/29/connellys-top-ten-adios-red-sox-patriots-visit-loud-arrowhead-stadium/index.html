Connelly’s Top Ten: Adios Red Sox; Patriots Visit Loud Arrowhead Stadium

By Michael in Featured, Now!, Top Story ⋅ September 29, 2014 at 6:54am ⋅ Post a comment

1. Red Sox Recap:

* Nava hit .270 – hats off to a great comeback year

* No Red Sox played 145 games, had 160 hits, scored 75 runs, only Ortiz had over 17 HR, Second to Ortiz in RBI was Napoli with 55, only one player had double digit SB

* Nineteen Red Sox had at least 90 at bats

* When an celebration event is orchestrated – you can’t have guys walking from the dugout to shortstop – the downtime kills the flow

* John Lackey led the team with wins with 11

* Clay Buchholz had 13 quality starts out of 28

* With Red Sox winning Saturday their draft pick went from 4th to 6th

* Napoli extension in the offseason was like the Mike Lowell extension after 2007 championship

* Season ended perfect – a loss and a 3:14 game

2. Ryder Cup:

* Bubba can’t be telling the crowd to make noise and then go 0-3 for the weekend

* How about Rose and Stenson shooting birdies on ten straight holes Saturday

* After two Ryder Cups Rickie Fowler hasn’t one a match yet

* That Jim Furyk must be a wildman to party with (Don’t call me Francis)

* Tom Watson was probably the best match player in USA colors

* Keegan Bradley and his high top Air Jordan cleats was benched by old school Watson

* I liked the visits and analysis by European players – Montgomerie, Clark and Faldo (especially when Faldo absolutely ripped Sergio)

* You know Tiger was secretly happy with the result

* Phil and Watson rift

3. Around MLB:

* Hats off for Altuve pulling the anti-Boggs and playing and getting 2 hits to secure his batting title

* The two batting champs Morneau and Altuve were great values at $5mm and $1.2mm respectively

* Since 1998, eight of seventeen batting titles in the National League have gone to Rockies

* No American League pitcher won 19 games

* Red Sox Christian Vazquez led baseball in throwing out 52% of the runners (qualified at 25 plus attempts)

* Only five of the top 12 teams in payroll made the playoffs

* Zimmerman no hitter 1-0 was 2:01 in time (amazing Yaz like catch in the ninth)

* Theo has to have a huge year in 2015 – Cubs from July 30th were 29-27

4. 2014 Predictions – from March 31, 2014 post:

* I said under on 88 wins

* I said under on Victorino playing 125 games

* I said Lester 16 wins – he had 16 wins

* I said under on Dodgers 95 wins – they had 94

5. Around College Football:

* Boston College can’t lose games like Saturday if they want people to take them serious

* I was 1-0-1 this weekend making me 1-5-1 for the season

* Charlie Weis fired – again (he should rethink his 60 minutes interview) Before interview 19-6 / After 22-43

* Notre Dame Quarterback Everett Golson is 16-0 in his last sixteen regular season games

6. Patriots preview:

* Loud noise at Arrowhead – Brady should borrow that shark cage from Richard Dreyfus

* Alex Smith can hurt you with his feet

* Chiefs averaging 3 sacks a game

* They have ZERO interceptions

* Frankie Hammond a problem on punt returns averaging 13.5 yards

* Dustin Colquitt averaging net 44.9 yards a punt

7. Amazing Stat:

Did I hear this right? The other night was the first game in twenty years in which Jeter played a game at Yankee Stadium without playoff consequences – wow!

8. Old School:

Quarterbacks at age 37 (Brady’s current age) – ranked by QB rating:

* Manning…68% completion / 55 TD to 10 INT / 115% QB Rating

* S Young…..62% completion / 36 TD to 12 INT / 101% QB Rating

* Warner…..67% completion / 30 TD to 14 INT / 97% QB Rating

* Elway……..57% completion / 27 TD to 11 INT / 88% QB Rating

* Montana..61% completion / 13 TD to 7 INT / 87% QB Rating

* Favre……..56% completion / 18 TD to 18 INT / 73% QB Rating

* Moon……..58% completion / 21 TD to 21 INT / 75% QB Rating

* Marino…..58% completion / 23 TD to 15 INT / 67% QB Rating

9. Just a Little Bit Outside:

Is this the worst year for the four local teams General Managers:

* Ainge didn’t pull off the trade and lost again on ping pong balls – staring at 30 win season

* Everything Cherrington touched died on the vine

* The Bruins are up against the cap and did nothing to a team that is trending down ( see Marchand and Lucic stats)

* Patriots so far don’t look like an upgrade from last year

10. Randomocity:

* There is no way Rondo fell in the shower – that injury is from punching something. I said three years ago to trade him!

* Ray Donovan’s wife has the worst Boston accent since Jeff Bridges in Blown Away

* Of the top 20 Quarterbacks in passing yards, only Marino, Brady, Elway, Eli, Roethlisberger and Jim Kelly played just for one team

* How come Tuukka was sick for the game against Canada in the Olympics – ooh my arm!

* Brandon Merriweather 13 tackles Sunday

* NFL kickers are an INSANE 9-13 from 50 yards and beyond

* Someone made a good point the other day on radio – Amendola is not producing because he is redundant to Edelman. They don’t need two slot guys

* Would Brady win Punt Pass and Kick with his inability to throw the ball down field

* Great point the other day on the PC police – should the Pentagon switch the name of our aggressive weapons and helicopter from Tomahawks and Apache
