Connelly’s Top Ten: Are You Ready for Some Football!

By Michael in Featured, Now!, Top Story ⋅ September 5, 2014 at 6:55am ⋅ Post a comment

1. Patriots Preview:

* Gronk over under on regular season games played 11 (under)

* Games Kraft is showed in – over / under 15 (easy I go over – although NFL Network has them on 10/16 and they snubbed him last year)

* Hoodies worn Over / Under 11 (I go under)

* Ridley fumbles Over / Under 5 (I go under)

* Brady sacked – 35 (Over)

* Second Leading Receiver Dobson, Gronk, Amendola, Wright (I go Wright)

* Leading yards from backfield (I go Vereen, Ridley then White)

* Average blood alcohol of Patriot fan 0.10 (I’m going over)

* Average blood alcohol of Patriot mascot 0.20 (I’m going over)

* Times after a sack the announcers talk about Dante Scarnecchia – 3 (over)

* Go 12-4 and Reeves makes us remember Mike Haynes, Raymond Clayborn and Ty Law all in one!

* Browner over / under on personal fouls 6 (over)

2. NFL Predictions:

* Pete Carroll isn’t pumped and jacked at end of the season

* Tom Coughlin’s face turns red so much that he is let go at end of season

* Redskins aren’t named by at least half of the announcers but instead just Washington

* Chip Kelly goes backwards in Philly – Nick Foles more interceptions than TD

* Suh has to attend rage anonymous with George Constanza

* Atlanta goes to Super Bowl 11-5

* Jim Harbaugh last year in San Fran

* Rex Ryan again splits with Pats as he outcoaches the Pats

* Welker doesn’t make it to postseason but is high as a cut in Denver

3. Red Sox:

* Red Sox GM missed again by holding on to Uehara – yikes – bad year for both of them

* Have the Red Sox completely mis-managed the development of Jackie Bradley and Will Middlebrooks

* Speaking of Middlebrooks, he struck out looking FOUR times the other night (f-ing compete!) – has to be a record (he has struck out 19 of last 44 at bats)

* How would you like to play hard for Portland Seadogs all year and lead the team to the playoffs and you get bounced from the starting line up by Cuban Rusney Castillo

* Christian Vazquez – in 40 games only 21 runners have tried to steal on him – he’s thrown out 10 of them – WOW!

4. Around the MLB – Unjuiced?

* Three batters are on pace to hit 40 home runs this year (Stanton, Carter, Cruz) / in 2000, sixteen hitters hit 40 plus

* Fifteen batters are on pace to hit over .300 this year / 2000 there were 49 hitters over .300

* Thirteen batters are on pace to knock in 100 RBI this year / 2000 53 hitters had 100 RBI +

5. Worthy or Not:

Luis Aparico is in the Hall of Fame – NOT WORTHY

* Career .262 hitter / .311 OBP

* Career 83 HR

* Career – had 366 Errors (366!)

6. Great vs. Great:

In Game 7 of the NBA 1974 finals – Dave Cowens gave up six inches of height to Kareem but outdueled him to victory:

Cowens – 28 points / 14 rebounds

Kareem – 26 points / 13 rebounds

7. Amazing Stat:

George Brett only had 200+ hits twice in his career and never had over 30 home runs in a season / Henry Aaron stole 28 bases at age 34 / Robert Clemente never had 30 home runs in a season

8. Old School:

Last Bruins MVP – Phil Esposito -1974

9. Just a Little Bit Outside:

My top ten movies over the last ten years:

1. Capote

2. Slumdog Millionaire

3. Michael Clayton

4. Zero Dark Thirty

5. Kings Speech

6. Intouchables

7. Wedding Crashers

8. Argo

9. Hangover

10. Cinderella Man

Honorable Mention – Night at the Museum, Invincible, Midnight in Paris, Hugo, the Descendents

10. Randomocity:

* She sounds hideous

* College Football – Game of the Week Stanford – USC (Stanford easy) / Covering San Jose State getting 29.5 over Auburn – San Jose State is a quality team

* Stephen Drew 11-77 with Yankees

* In Jeter’s last game at Yankee Stadium should they bring out all of his former girlfriends like all of the character witnesses in the finale of Seinfeld

* Tennis is desperate for Monfils to make a run

* Getting a little excited for Ryder Cup

* Toughest season ticket in sports next year will be the Cubs with four rookies fighting for Rookie of the Year they are 13-6 since bringing two of them up

* Would Steve Austin have tested positive?
