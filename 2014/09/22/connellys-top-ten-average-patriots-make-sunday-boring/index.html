Connelly’s Top Ten: Average Patriots Make Sunday Boring

By Michael in Featured, Now!, Top Story ⋅ September 22, 2014 at 6:51am ⋅ Post a comment

1. Patriots Survive Loser Oakland:

MISC

* Boy are they boring

* Patriots were in last place two weeks ago and now in first

* Evan Washburn on the sideline for CBS confirming Pam Oliver’s claim that you have to be blond and pretty to be on the sideline

* 70% of red seats empty at kickoff

* Amazing this is Belichick’s 15th season with the Patriots

* The effort of Matthew Slater is a joy to watch

OFFENSE

* Another boring, uncreative, disconnected and failed offensive game plan

* Yet again the play action, fake end around almost got Brady killed

* Nate Solder should be embarrassed

* Three and out again on the first drive

* I think Brady gets predictable with his cadence

* On Brady’s first pass that was incomplete that Lafell fell out of the break you could hear the QB F Bomb

* Every time Gronk runs after the catch you hold your breath

* Brady was too cute with the clock at the end of the first half (it’s a game for him to leave no time left on the clock)

* Whenever they hurry up to the line to rush a play – they run left 85% of the time and it seldom works

* Another penalty on Lafell for a pick play – go to acting school for crying out loud

DEFENSE

* Revis Island is looking like a good place for receivers to visit so far

* I love the little dive by Wilfork after he intercepted the pass – like his first day of swimming lessons

* KRAFT SIGHTINGS – It was a Kraftapoolooza Sunday

* Three games – shown in all three games

* Shown with 6:08 left in the second quarter with Marky Mark (like the hat Mark of Pat Patriot but lose the sticker)

* Shown after Gronk TD begging for a high five from Marky Mark

* Shown with 2:44 left in the half awarding money’s for Hispanic month

* Was actually part of the halftime highlight package on CBS

STATS

* Amendola has 57 catches in 19 potential games

* Three touchdown drives in three games

* Gronk 6 TD’s in his last seven games (not including Cleveland, which he was hurt in)

2. Around the NFL

* I think Cincy is a good pick for the AFC championship 8-1 odds

* With ISIS looking to a make a big splash NFL players better be careful in London this week

3. Red Sox and around MLB

* Joe Kelly is a decent #4

* Blue Jay Buerhle started 10-1 up to June 1st, since 2-9

* Stephen Drew down to .163 for the Yankees

* Felix Dubront is 2-1 / 3.98 ERA for the Cubs

* Nationals pitcher Gio Gonzalez year before being “caught” for juice 21-8 / after 20-18

4. Around College Football

* Sports fans are watching the middle stages of the evolution of an eventual problem for society in Jamis Winston – accusations of a sexual crime,

* Didn’t take long for Nick Saban to go off on offensive coordinator Lane Kiffin

5. Worthy or Not:

Is Revis worthy of his title or another star that came to town on the back nine:

* Paul Coffey

* Guy Lapointe

* Jim Schonfeld

* Joe Kapp

* Artis Gilmore

* Dave Bing

* Pete Maravich

* Rogie Vachon

6. Andy Dalton caught a TD pass:

Patriot QBs with receptions:

* Steve Grogan – 3

* Tom Brady – 1

* Drew Bledsoe – 1

* Tom Hodson – 1

* Matt Cavanaugh – 1

7. Amazing Stat:

Amendola gets $387,500 a game or a $129,166 a catch

8. Old School:

At his rate Edelman will catch the greatest % of the quarterback’s completions in a season than any other Patriots receiver:

* 2014 – Edelman 22 catches of Brady’s 67 completions 33%

* 1996 – Coates 96 catches of Bledsoe’s 400 completions 24%

* 1986 – Morgan 84 catches of Eason/Grogran’s 340 completions 25%

* 1973 – Reggie Rucker 53 catches of Plunkett’s 193 completions 27%

* 1966 – Artie Graham 51 catches of Babe Parelli’s 181 completions 28%

9. Just a Little Bit Outside:

Watched three classics this weekend – All the President’s Men, Walking Tall and One Flew Over the Cuckoo’s Nest. I would put Jack Nicholson’s performance in top five of all time:

1. George C Scott – Patton

2. Philip Seymour Hoffman – Capote

3. Ralph Fiennes – Schindlers List

4. Robert Deniro – Raging Bull

5. Jack Nicholson – One Flew Over the Cuckoo’s Nest

10. Randomocity:

* Summer is over – best summer bands – Beach Boys, Go-Go’s, B 52’s

* Who is a worse Managers Rueben Kinkaid or John Farrell

* Underrated – deep-sea fishing and bowling

* Ryder Cup around the corner – American’s will get up early only to come up short on putts on Sunday
