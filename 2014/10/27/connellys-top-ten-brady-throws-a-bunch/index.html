Connelly’s Top Ten: Brady Throws A Bunch

By Michael in Featured, Now!, Top Story ⋅ October 27, 2014 at 6:49am ⋅ Post a comment

1. Patriots:

MISC

* You know it’s an announcer that doesn’t have the Patriots much when he says “Brady has so many weapons.”

* Lot of empty seats at kickoff and many of them got a quick jump on halftime actually missed two touchdowns

* Like Chara too much of Gronk in October doesn’t bode well for playoff time

* Surprised no one from Cambridge is petitioning the State House for stricter gun laws with all the musket fire in Foxboro

OFFENSE

* Pats scored on their first drive again

* Brady only five incomplete passes – two included drops by Edelman

* Gostkowski is 18-19 (one miss a bad snap)

* When the Patriots hurry up to the line near the goal line they fail 90% of the time

* They tried the play action / fake end around again – no success again.

* Great game by offensive line and obviously the QB (148.4 QB rating – sounds good)

* Not for nothing but last years Receiver cache of Dobson, Thompkins and Boyce have a combined 3 receptions this year

* Brady doesn’t even look at Amendola (by the way him returning punts is a concussion waiting to happen)

* Like what Jonas Gray brings – getting to the hole with purpose

DEFENSE

* I thought Browner looked good – refs were to his advantage (only two penalties)

* Jamie Collins might have led the team in tackles but seemed to be chasing all afternoon

* Dominique Easley showed a little bit

* Is Ninkovich not holding the edge this year?

* The announcer was saying how valuable Ninkovich is because he plays all the time – is that like eating up innings?

BEARS

* Anything more pathetic than Lamarr Houston’s sack dance while getting block out and be carted off the field?

BOB KRAFT – He is on fire:

* Shown at 8:09 of second quarter

* Shown kissing Vince at 6:50 of the third quarter

* With 0:54 left in the third quarter a whole homage to him under the graphic Era of Excellence

* With 0:45 left in the third quarter yet again

2. Around the NFL:

* After watching Denver Thursday night it is possible that they are better at every position but tight end and kicker?

* 49ers and Cincy are the only teams with a winning record but have scored fewer points than opponents

* The poor Jets won their opener too – quite a tease for Fireman Bill

* The entire AFC North has a winning record / the entire NFC South have a losing record

* Seeing Roethlisberger with stripes on is so appropriate

* Don’t tell me that Gino Smith is better than Tim Tebow

3. Around College Football:

* Picks of the week – I was 3-1-1 this week bringing me to 11-9 so far this year (or a 11-4 since bad start)

* Why not to bet reason #3,621,435, with Kentucky scoring to cover with seconds left they tried to onside kick and Mississippi State didn’t only recover but ran it back for a touchdown

* Les Miles of LSU upset undefeated Ole Miss bringing him to 46-3 on Saturday Nights in Baton Rouge (from ESPN he is also 26-25 when trailing in the 4th quarter)

* TCU scored 82 points against Texas Tech – shouldn’t someone get fired?

* My prediction that the Falcons are going to Super Bowl is not looking good

4. World Series:

* Fox has virtually been comparing Salvador Perez catching 147 games to building the Great Wall of China – in 1978 Carlton Fisk played in 157 games

* Games times so far 3:32 /3:25 / 3:15 / 4:00

* Dan Shaughnessy wrote about the 1962 Game 7 between Giants and Yankees – Game time 2:29 / date October 16th

* Royals that are paid more than Red Sox’ Rusney Castillo’s 2015 salary of $10.5mm – James Shields ($13.5mm) and Alex Gordon ($11.25mm)

5. Garoppolo:

Jimmy Garoppolo is 55 for 89 in the regular season and exhibition season 62% completion

6. Revolution:

Hats over to the Revolution who qualified for the playoffs and drew 32,766 Saturday – star of the tema is Lee Nguyen who has 18 goals

7. Amazing Stat:

Patriots ROAD record since 2001 – 71-33 with only one losing season on the road 2009 when they were 2-6

8. Old School:

Gronk vs Winslow

Winslow vs. Miami 1981 – 13 receptions / 166 yards / 1 TD / 1 blocked field goal

Gronk vs. Da Bears 2014 – 9 receptions / 149 yards / 3 TD

10. Randomocity:

* Lost on Middlebrooks not catching the throw against St. Louis at third was the fact that Pedroia made the greatest defensive play in World Series history

* If the industry of bowling is hurting enough – 1 guy with Ebola went bowling in New York last week

* the fact that Zedeno Chara has an actual tear I think means more than four weeks

* I don’t think the controversy is that Toys R Us got rid of the Breaking Bad action figures – it is that they had them in the first place

* In Conde’ Nast Traveler, XV Beacon of Boston is rated the top hotel in the United States

* Finished the book The Boys in the Boat about the eight man USA Crew team in the 1936 Olympics – A – / very good story and extremely well written

* Inconsistent – NCAA has ruled that Mo’ne Davis will not lose her amateur status despite being compensated for her Chevy commercial (money into a trust fund)

* Three saddest movie songs – Brian Song, Deer Hunter, Terms of Endearment
