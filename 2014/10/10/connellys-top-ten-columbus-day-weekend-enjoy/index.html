Connelly’s Top Ten: Columbus Day Weekend – Enjoy

By Michael in Featured, Now!, Top Story ⋅ October 10, 2014 at 7:42am ⋅ Post a comment

1. Patriots Preview – @ Buffalo Bills:

* Brandon Spikes is working up to a foam

* Freddy Jackson has over 250 yards after the catch (I love his game)

* It’s amazing how much better the Bills skilled weapons are – Sammy Watkins, Fred Jackson, CJ Spiller, Robert Woods, Scott Chandler (its not even close)

* Bills already have 17 sacks – Marcell Dareus has 5

* Kicker Dan Carpenter is 4-5 from 40 yards out (including 58 yarder last week to win it)

* Team only has one rushing TD

* Patriots are 65-41-1 against Bills

* At least three time All Pros in Bills history: 8 x Bruce Smith / 5 x Billy Shaw / OJ 5 / 3 x Mike Stratton / George Saimes / Joe Delamielleure / Butch Byrd / Tom Sestak

2. MLB Playoffs:

* Payrolls: 7. Giants – $154mm / 13. Cardinals – $111mm / 15. Orioles – $107mm / 19. Royals $92mm

* OBP rank: 9 – Cardinals / 16 – Royals / 17. Orioles / 18. Giants

* Attendance rank: 2. Cardinals / 4. Giants / 13. Orioles / 25th Royals

3. Tom Brady:

Brady is on pace for 17 fumbles and 8 interceptions

4. Around College Football:

* Games of the Week: Auburn vs Mississippi State / TCU vs. Baylor / Oregon vs. UCLA / Ole Miss vs. Texas A&M

* Pick of the Week Texas A&M over Ole Miss giving two points

* Gurley suspension might have just cost him the Heisman

5. Football suspensions so far this year:

Ray Rice, Adrian Peterson, Todd Gurley, Jamis Winston

6. Brad Marchand:

He has now gone fourteen games without scoring

7. Old School:

Luis Tiant complete games from 1973 to 1976 compared to Red Sox pitching staff the last four years:

* Tiant – 23 / 25 / 18 / 19

* Staff – 2 / 6 / 5 / 3

8. Amazing Stats:

Ron Guidry 1978 vs Clayton Kershaw 2014

Pitcher……….W-L….ERA……CG…….K………WHIP

GUIDRY………25-3….1.77…..16…….248…..0.857

KERSHAW…..21-3….1.74…….6…….239…..0.946

9. Just a Little Bit Outside:

Has the attack already started:

* Oklahoma beheading

* Unknown sicknesses with over a 1,000 afflicted in Seattle, Colorado, Alabama and fifteen other states

* Fire at 9/11 site in Pennsylvania

10. Randomocity:

*Kenyan runner Dennis Kimetto ran a 2:02:57 at the Berlin Marathon this month – thats a 4:41 PACE for 26 straight miles

* Ovechkin finally made it to the cover of SI

* Saw a real good sports documentary – Undefeated, which won the Oscar – worth the watch (Netflix)

* Tiant would get the ball before Kershaw

* Have the Celtics and Bruins ever been so collectively irrelevant in October?

* SNL actress Jan Hooks dies – imagine the actuary tables to insure stars on that show
