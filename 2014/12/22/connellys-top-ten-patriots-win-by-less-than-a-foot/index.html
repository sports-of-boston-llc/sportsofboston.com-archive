Connelly’s Top Ten: Patriots Win By Less Than a FOOT

By Michael in Featured, Now!, Top Story ⋅ December 22, 2014 at 6:49am ⋅ Post a comment

1.PATRIOTS:

MISC

* Gray hoodie with cut sleeves and pom pom on hat – who can beat that

* Big penalty on Bolden, taking back a nice return for Amendola

* Jets destroyed both lines of scrimmage – thank God for Revis

* The whole game was a Rex Ryan fest even thought he lost the game, he’s getting fired, has a career losing record

OFFENSE

* Offensive line terrible again as well as scheming – Brady hit 11 times

* No Blount, no Edelman – it showed

* I’m no NFL Coach BUT a hurry up offense seems to have a negative impact upon a pass rushing team

* Not Gronk’s best game – a couple of drops, not great in pass protection, was taken down by a single tackler a couple of times

* Brady another double digit yard run

* I think Tim Wright is under utilized

* I saw Vereen break a tackle

* Only one screen

DEFENSE

* Jamie Collins has turned into an All-Pro

* Vince moving even better again doing his thing

* Chung a horrendous day – missed tackles, bad angles, didn’t contain on the option pitch and burnt for a touchdown

KRAFT SIGHTING

* 2nd Quarter – 9:05 left with Bon Jovi who serves as Mr. Kraft’s posy

* 4th Quarter – 13:50 remaining (Jonathan doing a commercial for Isotoner?)

STATS

* Amendola 217 total yards and a big conversion on the first down that wasn’t a first down

* Ryan Allen stays red hot averaging 52.6 yards

* Brady now 21-6 against the Jets

* Fourth time that Amendola has more than five catches in a game for the Pats

OTHER

* Nice of Woody Johnson to do the Christmas wish with his daughter and grandkids (with hat on)

* Refs missed a crazy face mask on Amendola that ripped his helmet off

* No penalties for Browner

2. Around the NFL:

* Kansas City Chiefs wide receivers still haven’t caught a TD this year

* Miami comeback saved their coach his job

* My Atlanta team marches on toward the Super Bowl

* Watching the end of the Washington – Philly game makes you appreciate Bill Belichick even

* Flacco struggling after Obama called him out for his appearance in the Anti-North Korean movie with 29 incompletions

* At $22mm a year, Cutler earned over a $1mm Sunday for sitting on the bench

* Johnny Manziel inglorious beginning – Hoyer must be loving it

* Good playoff consequence games next week – Carolina – Atlanta / Green Bay – Detroit

3. Around College Football:

* Great season for UNH but if quarterback Sean Goldrich hit on one of the three overthrows to RJ Harris all alone behind the secondary they would be going to the finals

* Yesterday would have been the perfect day for the NCAA quarterfinals – giving the winners ten days to New Years

5. Orr > Gretzky:

Why Bobby Orr is better than the floater Wayne Gretzky reason #6,213,421 – in the seasons following their last meaningful season with their team

* Bruins didn’t win a Stanley Cup for almost four decades

* Two years after he left – the 1989 Oilers won the Stanley Cup without him

7. Amazing Stats:

Patriot running back Andy Johnson had as many touchdown passes as Rohan Davey, Scott Secules and Michael Bishop combined – 4

8. Old School:

The first down by Amendola to clinch a one-point win in the Meadowlands reminded me of Ben Coates stretching for a first down against the Giants 19 years ago on the same day to get them into the playoffs. Dave Meggett returned a 60-yard punt for a 23-22 victory

9. Just a Little Bit Outside:

Battle of the Bulge occurred 70 years ago from December 16th to January 25th (Wikipedia)

* 610,000 American soldiers / 89,000 casualties

* The depiction of the battle in Band of Brothers will make you shiver.

* Battle of the Bulge movie starred Henry Fonda, Robert Shaw, Telly Savalas, Charles Bronson did $4.5mm at the box office

* Temperatures were said to go as low as ten below zero.

10. Randomocity:

*Larry Bird never missed a big free throw ever

* Surprised that more soccer players don’t hit their heads on the goal posts

* Remember when people were uttering comparisons of Evan Longoria about Will Middlebrooks
