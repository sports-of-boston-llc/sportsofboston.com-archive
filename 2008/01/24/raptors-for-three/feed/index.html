Indeed an interesting idea. They are basically setting up a futures market for ticket sales. It works for the team/ticketing site since your “bet” is non-refundable and only serves to guarantee you the ability to purchase your tickets (at face value). It would be interesting to see if the insurance bet on the ticket changes based on how well/poorly the team does for the remainder of the season. It’s a risk, and you’d need to factor your insurance bet into the price, but on the other hand, you wouldn’t have to end up paying an obscene price to a scalper. Interesting idea! I’d like to follow it to see how the prices change for the different teams.
