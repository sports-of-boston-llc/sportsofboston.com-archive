Connelly’s Top Ten – One Week to Go!

By Michael in Uncategorized ⋅ January 29, 2017 at 6:51pm ⋅ Post a comment

1.Patriots Stuff

  • Now we can start getting excited
  • Saw an article that asked if Matt Ryan is the next Tom Brady? What – Matt Ryan is 31 and has a 3-4 playoff record. By that time Brady was 31 – he had three rings and a playoff record of 14-3
  • Since video-gate Tom Brady is 125-35 – (don’t forget videotaping other teams’ signals is not illegal – you just can’t do it from the sideline)
  • A goofy observation – Brady is 4-0 versus team named after an animal – Rams, Eagles, Panthers, Seahawks
  • Brady is 4-0 against the Falcons
  • Patriots have lost three first round picks during the Belichick run – one for the trade to the NYJ, videogate, deflategate

 

  1. Australian Open

* Average age of four finalists- Williams sisters / Federer and Nadal – 34

* Combined grand slam championships –  62

* Does the ball boy get tipped for bringing a towel to the competitors after every point

* How does Nadal wear a watch while playing

* Front row seats went for $24,000

 

  1. Shelf life of Edelman using Welker as gauge – probably got two more years:

Welker Six years – 556 receptions / 926 targets

Edelman four years – 356 receptions / 532 targets

 

  1. Local teams being dominated:

Bruins against Montreal – 0-18 in playoff series from 1943-1988

Patriots in Miami – 0-16 in games from 1970-1985

 

  1. Is Gronk a Hall of Famer if he never plays another game. YES! Below he is compared against some other Hall of Fame tight ends and also Lynn Swann who had a quick but brilliant career (don’t forget Gronk is one of the great blockers from his position):

Gronk………………405 Rec / 6,095 yards / 15.0 avg yd / 69 TD

Dave Casper………..378 Rec / 5,216 yards / 13.8 avg yd / 52 TD

Kellen Winslow…… 541 Rec / 6,741 yards / 12.5 avg yd /45 TD

Jackie Smith………..480 Rec / 7,918 yards / 16.5 avg yd / 40 TD

 

Gronk………88 games / 405 Rec / 6,095 yards / 15.0 avg yd / 69 TD

Lynn Swann 116 games / 336 Rec / 5,462 yards / 16.3 avg yd /  52 TD

 

  1. Mount Rushmore of current local players most likely to go the Hall of Fame – Tom Brady, Patrice Bergeron, Gronk, maybe Pedroia

  1. AMAZING STAT – – From Either – Russell, Bird, Flutie, Orr, Williams, Pedro, Brady, Neely stat – Neely had 176 goals over 216 games from 1990-1994

JUST A LITTLE BIT OUTSIDE – Bill Belichick when hired by Cleveland in 1991:

  • Youngest head coach at the time – age 38
  • Bobby Knight called Art Modell to provide a reference
  • Belichick said at the press conference: “I’m looking for players who put themselves behind the team, unselfish players. I believe in a team effort
  • Got the job over Bill Cowher – defensive coordinator of Kansas City
  • Browns owner Art Modell on the hire – “It’s a gamble….maybe I’ve struck gold on this one.”
  1. RANDOMOCITY

* Right now, the #1 and #2 picks in the NBA draft would go to the Celtics and then the Lakers

* Sports Illustrated ranked the most iconic moments in NHL history – #1 was Bobby Orr’s goal – boy have we been spoiled

* Golden State scored 86 points in two quaarters

* No on – Sicilian pizza

* Al Horford on pace to play 61 games which means he gets $442,622 a game

* Atlanta is getting new baseball field – the old one was 25 years old and a new football stadium which was only twenty years old
