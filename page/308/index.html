Aaron Hernandez’s Fiancee Indicted on Perjury Charge

September 28, 2013 at 10:28am in Featured, Patriots, Top Story
Ying Tao writes
Add a comment

A grand jury has indicted former Patriots tight end Aaron Hernandez’s fiance on a perjury charge in connection with the June murder of Odin Lloyd. According to Bristol County District Attorney Samuel Sutter, a grand jury indicted Shayanna Jenkins on a single count in relation to the Lloyd murder investigation.

It is unclear what led to the charge but according to court documents released last month, Jenkins had become an interest to investigators because they believed she knew the location of the gun used to kill Lloyd. Authorities searched a storage unit rented under her name and also have surveillance video of what they believe is her getting rid of evidence related to the case, specifically a .22-caliber gun they later discovered in the woods. Police also have text messages Hernandez sent mentioning to Jenkins about a box in the basement an hour before she is seen leaving with a heavy object. Tim Thomas Signs with Florida Panthers

September 26, 2013 at 8:07pm in Bruins, Featured
KC Downey writes
Add a comment

Former Bruins goalie Tim Thomas is back in the NHL, this time with the Florida Panthers.

Thomas, 39, signed a one-year deal, according to ESPN. The deal could be worth up to $3.75 million, with incentives.

Thomas took last season off because he felt he needed a break, but it would appear he’s ready to return to the NHL.

He played his entire career with the Bruins, winning a Stanley Cup in 2011.

He famously declined an invitation from President Barack Obama to go to the White House to celebrate the win.

Winning the Wire and Surviving the Bye: Fantasy Football Week 4 Sleepers

September 25, 2013 at 7:33am in Fantasy, Featured, Top Story
Kevin O'Keefe writes
Add a comment

Week 4 of the season means our opinions of teams and players are starting to be based on 2013 evidence.  There is a lot of football to play, and most of us hope some high draft picks pick it up (I’m talking to you C.J. Spiller).  It also means the bye weeks are here and this week leaves us without the Panthers and Packers.  At least two teams are likely playing backup quarterbacks this week and from now through week 12 owners will have to drop good players and juggle rosters.  Week 1 and 2 darlings James Starks and Eddie Royal flamed out for different reasons in Week 3, and other players stepped up.  Last week, I hit on Ryan Tannehill, Jason Snelling, Danny Woodhead (PPR special), and Nate Burleson.  Royal and the TE selections were misses along with Alex Smith, who played good real football and bad fantasy football.  Minnesota’s DST was serviceable.  Relevant players out due to the bye are: Aaron Rodgers, Randall Cobb, Jordy Nelson, James Jones, Jermichael Finley (also hurt), Cam Newton, Steve Smith, Greg Olsen, and DeAngelo Williams.
