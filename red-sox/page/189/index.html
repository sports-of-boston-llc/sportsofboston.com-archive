The nightmarish September of the Red Sox only got worse on Saturday as they were demolished by the New York Yankees 9-1.

The trouble started with one out in the second inning. Robinson Cano hit a bouncer just past the dive of shortstop Marco Scutaro for a single. Lester then walked Nick Swisher, bringing up Andruw Jones.
