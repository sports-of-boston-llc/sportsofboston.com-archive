Connelly’s Top Ten: Manning & Manningham Yes – Brady & Welker No!

By Michael in Featured, Now!, Patriots, Top Story ⋅ February 6, 2012 at 7:00am ⋅ Post a comment

Everyday we do our best to fight the good fight. We go to work, survive bosses, tolerate traffic, make sincere efforts to parent to the best of our abilities, do our best to appease spouses, pay the bills, go to sleep and then wake up and do the same thing over again. To do more than just subsist, we must be able to attach ourselves to something that distinguishes us from others.

The Patriots and primarily Tom Brady and Bill Belichick up to this point, have given us the ability to feel special and unique. Their successes have allowed us, up to this point, to say that we, in some small way, we are part of something that is extraordinary. But, now we’re just fans of  another really good team. Somewhere, Joe Montana and Vince Lombardi can go to sleep Sunday night with their legacy secure while we replay what could have been and wonder why we’re not fans of the greatest football team ever. Like the groundhog not seeing his shadow – the Patriots losing means our winter will be longer and we’re no longer special!

1. Random Thoughts

* Tom Brady only player that came out without a helmet
* I guess the Red Hoodie wasn’t the difference in the 2007 outcome
* Both QB’s forgot to shave
* Should the Patriots be allowed to pick up a player less than 24 hours before the Super Bowl?
* Tom Coughlin and Tom Brady sang the national anthem (most players, I’m sure, don’t know the words)
* Without question the play to Wes Welker with 4:00 left – is the play of the game
* Three fumbles by the Giants and the Pats recovered NONE!
* Starting to think that Boston sports magic is fading away
* Bad challenge by Belichick costing the team a timeout – I think his record down the stretch was average on challenges
* Square 9-0 came up for the first and second quarter
* Think the intentional grounding is a questionable call in the Super Bowl
* They had to win the turnover battle (they lost) / Brady needed a 100% QB rating – 91% / They couldn’t give up 30 yard plays they gave up one BIG one / Less than 2 sacks – they gave up 2
* First Kraft sighting was third quarter (late)
* Worst thing is that nationally they will say – Patriots never won without SpyGate
* Vegas got killed / local bookies clean up
* Jets fans, Montana, Steeler fans, 49ers fans – and damn Giants fans celebrating
* Four of the five Patriots Super Bowls have been determined in the last minute
* Brady will have surgery on that left shoulder

2. OFFENSE

* Danny Woodhead had a huge game (I didn’t think they should dress him instead of Kevin Faulk) but his first down late on the swing pass could have been the game
* Doesn’t the offensive line want to cut some of those defensive linemen?
* Brady should have taken a timout earlier in the last-second drive of the first half
* If Tom Brady leads Deion Branch with a minute left, it’s a touchdown
* Logan Mankins in back-to-back Super Bowls looking like Max Lane– should be less “tough guy” and better blocker
* Ochocinco had a catch – gives him 16 in 18 games
* Thought some Red Zone play calling was conservative
* Gronkowski can’t allow that interception – and Brady has to throw that deeper
* Patriots didn’t score the last 26:20 of the game (four drives: punt, interception, punt, Hail Mary)

3. DEFENSE:

* I said one play over 30 yards and they lose, and the 38-yard pass to Manningham was it
* Sterling Moore is Dave Henderson – clutch plays in loss that will go forgotten
* Offsides by Rob Nickovich was Bill Buckner-esque on third down and incomplete
* Thought Devin McCourty had a good game and secured the starting safety position
* Twelve men on the field huge play in the game – cost them a fumble and points
* Why did Jerod Mayo have his back to the football on the slant touchdown to Victor Cruz?
* Too bad Vince Wilfork didn’t make the flight

4. NBC Broadcast (including commercials):

* Does Chris Collinsworth dislike the Patriots?
* Best commercials – kid peeing in pool / Camaro commercial for high school graduate
* NFL and NBC and the federal government should make performers sign a contract of conduct to perform with decorum at America’s greatest showcase – M.I.A. should be put in jail for the flipping the bird
* David Beckham commercial looked like a junkie in Fruit of the Looms – go back to the fruit guys

5. New York Giants:

* Tom Coughlin: His team, like expected, was well-prepared. New York media wanted him fired six weeks ago…
* Giants started 2007 with a nine-minute drive / this year a six-minute drive
* Eli Manning – Yet another QB with a QB rating over 100 (103.8) – the pass to Manningham was insane
* Salsa dancer – Pats D did a nice job on Cruz except for the TD
* At one point in the game, Antrel Rolle made a tackle after a Patriot first down and got up and celebrated
* Ahmad Bradshaw would have been the biggest goat in sports history if Patriots came back and won after falling into the end zone

6. Officials:

* They did a fine job – although the intentional grounding was not the time or place.
* Sterling Moore’s tip got Coughlin’s jugular to protrude, but it was a fair call

7. Stats:

* Turnovers: 1 to 0
* QB Rating: Brady needed 100 rating and came up with 91.1
* Time of Possession: Giants 37:05 / Patriots 22:55
* 81 total passes to 47 rushes

8. How proposals worked out:

* Player to win MVP: Eli Manning
* Player to score first touchdown: Victor Cruz
* Eli’s & Brady’s First pass: Reception (started 9 for 9) / Intentional Grounding
* Kelly Clarkson National Anthem1:340/U:1:33for National Anthem
