Connelly’s Top Ten: We Are Spoiled!

By Michael in Featured, Now! ⋅ January 20, 2012 at 7:00am ⋅ Post a comment

1. Patriots Preview:

* Don’t forget – appreciate (no…cherish!) every time Tom Brady walks onto the field representing all of us from New England
* Good thing that Baltimore is playing on the road – we don’t have to watch that stupid Ray Lewis dance
* Guarantee – Baltimore goes deep on their first play
* Don’t forget Anquan Boldin chose Baltimore over Patriots
* Hats off to the Patriots crowd last week – got raves from Patriots players – better bring it this week
* I predict the over-under for sick days on Monday in New England at 14% of the workforce
* Stevan Ridley will not see the ball if the Patriots have the lead
* Wes Welker: 10-plus catches / Danny Woodhead could be star of the game
* Bill goes with gray hoodie, cut sleeves, ear warmers
* Terrell Suggs is in the same sentence with Joey Porter and Michael Strahan – big mouths to shut up

2. NFC Championship:

* How come Joe Biden doesn’t take half the grief Dan Quayle did?
* I think the Giants are the fad pick now – I will go contra with 49ers – Frank Gore for 125 + yards
* Has a coach ever taken his college team to the top 4 and then brought his pro team to the top 4 the next year like Jim Harbaugh?
* Drew Brees, Peyton Manning, Matt Ryan, Aaron Rodgers, Phil Rivers at home watching Joe Flacco and Alex Smith (Sanchez is at a Junior high dance)

3. The First Big Three:

As we witness the fall of the second Big Three – lets remember how good the first Big Three was:

* From 1979 to 1987 the Celtics averaged 62 wins over eight years
* During these eight years, they played in the Finals five times

4. Tim Thomas:

Is Tim Thomas approaching greatest Bruins goalie status? I say second:

Goalie W-L (%) Vezinas Stanley Cups GAA
Tiny Thompson 252-153 (.622) 4 1 1.99
Tim Thomas 179-111 (.617) 2 1 2.46
Frank Brimsek 230-144 (.615) 0 2 2.58
Gerry Cheevers 229-101 (.693) 0 2 2.89

5. BC Football:

How can’t Boston College football recruit? Great campus, great school, near a thriving metropolis…..

* Have recruited four top 100 ranked high school players in ten years: Brian Toal ranked 25th – 2004 / Ryan Poles 47th – 2003 / Dorien Bryant 75th – 2003 / Jim Unis 100th – 2002
* Currently not ranked in the top 50 in the country in recruiting this year

6. CAPTAIN Paul Pierce so far this season:

* Showed up hurt
* Is shooting 37% from the field
* Averaging 2.7 turnovers a game
* Is playing 31 minutes out of 48 (64%) despite being paid $186,992 a game or $6,032 a minute

7. Amazing Stats:

Tom Brady stuff…

* 15-5 record in playoffs
* Career Quarterback rating by month: Sept – 96% / Oct – 98% / Nov – 97% / Dec – 95% / Jan – 93%
* Has won 69% of his games on the road (55-25)
* Has thrown 300 TD’s in 161 games (1.86)
* Completes 64% of his passes on the road and at home
* Has won 88% of his December games
* Is 22-5 on Monday, Thursday and Saturday
* He completes 63.8% of passes against the AFC / 63.8% against the NFC
* He is 30-0 against Eagles, Vikings, Tampa Bay, Seattle, Jacksonville, Dallas, Bengals, Baltimore, Falcons, Cardinals (regular season)

8. Old School:

Previous AFC Championship games for the Patriots

* 1963 – Loss -@ Chargers 51-10
* 1985 – Win – @ Miami 31-14
* 1996 – Win – Jacksonville 20-6
* 2001 – Win – Pittsburgh 24-17
* 2003 – Win – Indy 24-14
* 2004 – Win – @ Pittsburgh 41-27
* 2006 – Loss – @ Indy 38-34
* 2007 – Win – Chargers 21-12

Home – 4-0
Away – 2-2

9. Just a Little Bit Outside:

Old candies…

* Necco wafers
* Sugar Daddies (Sugar Babies are great but will challenge a filling)
* Turkish Taffy
* Charleston Chews
* Jiffy Pop
* Licorice
* Nonpareils
* Skybar

10. Randomocity:

* Holy **** did you see Taylor Hall’s wound after a teammate’s skate hit him in the head?
* Speaking of the original Boston Celtics Big Three team – I would say that only the Texas Western team of 1966 was more subjected to racist opinion than the 1985-1986 Celtic team
* A real good French Toast is better than waffles and pancakes
* Plea to Bill Belichick – take Tom Brady out of game in fourth quarter if up by 30 points
