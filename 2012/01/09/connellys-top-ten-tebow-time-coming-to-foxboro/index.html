Connelly’s Top Ten: Tebow Time Coming to Foxboro

By Michael in Featured, Now! ⋅ January 9, 2012 at 7:00am ⋅ Post a comment

1. Around the NFL playoffs

* I hate watching football or baseball played inside domes (all games outdoors next week)
* The goof playing the sax during the national anthem of the Giants game had his hat on
* I guess Matt Ryan is overrated
* Are you kidding me? Atlanta twice going 4th and 1 with quarterback sneaks against the strength of the Giants (their front line)
* Drew Brees will be tough to beat
* Did anyone care about the Houston game except bettors?
* Draft position of New Yorks line – Jason Pierre Paul 15th / Justin Tuck 74th / Chris Canty 132nd / Mathias Kiwanuka 32nd (lines up at linebacker) / Linval Joseph 46th / Osi Unenyiora 56th
* Is Tom Coughlin one of the most under-rated coaches in sports history?
* Roethlisberger is one tough football player (by the way he will be 350 pounds by age 48)
* Funny: CBS came out of a commercial with Charles Barkely for Weight Watchers right to 380-pound Steeler Casey Hampton on the sideline
* The Phil Simms obvious comment of the day: “a forward pass must go forward” – I’m still a big fan
* In the hurry-up offense and Pittsburgh rushing down the field to win, Antonio Brown caught a pass, got up and signaled first down and then flipped the ball forward, causing the refs to go get it and costing his team five seconds
* Why does John Elway come down on the field like Jerry Jones?
* CBS did a terrible job explaining the overtime rules – it sounded like each team had to get the ball at least once in overtime
* Ike Taylor better not go home to Pittsburgh
* Tebow averaged 31.6 yards a pass (Steve Smith said he couldn’t make his son’s Pop Warner team – hope he enjoyed watching from his living room)

2. Bruins:

* The Canuncks are so hateable
* So, 20 years ago Brad Park was said to be the league’s best hip-checker – and Marchand is now dirty?
* Howl watching Henrik Sedin jump on the bench as soon as the fight started

3. Patriots-Broncos:

The match-up and pre-game thoughts…

* Can the red seat crowd pull themselves away from the wine tasting and the fireplace for the first quarter at least?
* You listen to the Giants and Denver crowd, and there is no comparison
* Believe it or not, I would have rather seen Pittsburgh the way they’re beat up
* Tebow confidence versus Patriot secondary and pass rush – c’mon Tom Brady!

4. Things that are a waste of time in sports:

* The intentional walk
* The jump ball
* A kick off in Denver
* Touching up on icing
* An Eric Fernstein foul shot
* The bullpen coming in for a fight
* Watching Ortiz run down to first on a ground ball
* The extra point
* Watching the sixth game in tennis in a set that is being won 5-0

5. NFL coaches with career losing records:

Coach Record
Chan Gailey 28-36 (.438)
Marvin Lewis 69-74 (.483)
Norv Turner 107-113 (.489)
Jack Del Rio 68-71 (.489)
Gary Kubiak 47-49 (.490)
Pete Carroll 47-49 (.490)

6. Bill O’Brien:

Just throwing this out there – no one outside of New England had Bill O’Brien on their radar before the argument with Tom Brady. I’m not saying it was contrived, but it certainly enhanced his perception from a year previous when people said he was nothing but a puppet for Bill Belichick, who was calling the plays.

7. Amazing Stat:

The Boston Celtics two-year home record (1985-1987): 79-3 (like the Patriots and Tom Brady now – we were spoiled in the 80’s)

8. Old School:

Boston’s multiple MVP winners:

Player # of MVPs
Bill Russell 5
Eddie Shore 4
Bobby Orr 3
Larry Bird 3
Phil Esposito 2
Tom Brady 2
Ted Williams 2
Bill Cowley 2

* Cowley should have been on my most under-rated list

9. Just a Little Bit Outside:

My Oscar predictions to date…

I have seen the following Oscar movies to date to choose from (The Artist, The Descendents, Harry Potter, Win Win, Midnight in Paris, Bridesmaid, Money Ball)

* Best Movie – Win Win (the Artist will get it because it’s different)
* Best Actor – Brad Pitt for Money Ball
* Best Actress – None from the movies I’ve seen

10. Randomocity:

* The chest-bump might be the worst advancement of celebratory histrionics yet (a coach should be fined for participating)
* In general, I think Davios is overrated… BUT I got the steak and cheese and chicken parm spring rolls at Roche Brothers – best take home appetizer EVER!
* Disappointed in Homeland on Showtime
* When a coach has a decision at the end of the game to kick the extra point or go for two to win it, doesn’t the laws of probability favor going for two? What’s the odds of 2.5 yards with your best play in your playbook versus a 50/50 coin flip?
* Interesting piece on Tom Brady this week in Sports Illustrated
