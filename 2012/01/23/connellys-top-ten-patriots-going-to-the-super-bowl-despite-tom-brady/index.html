Connelly’s Top Ten: Patriots Going to the Super Bowl Despite Tom Brady

By Michael in Featured, Now! ⋅ January 23, 2012 at 7:00am ⋅ Post a comment

1. Patriots-Ravens:

How often do you get outplayed and lose the turnover battle and still win a football game?

OFFENSE

* Every time Patriots needed a big play on offense – Tom Brady failed
* Three times – Patriots had a chance to score touchdowns and settled for field goals (Brady over throwing a wide open Rob Gronkowski)
* Not a big fan of Deion Branch diving to avoid contact
* Nate Solder looked like Max Lane out there
* The greatest two-minute quarterback in the history of the game takes a knee at the end of the first half with two time outs? Playing not to lose!
* The fact that Stephen Gostowski had three field goals with a long of 35 yards – tells you the Patriots were settling for field goals
* Why didn’t Bill Belichick challenge the Gronkowski non-catch down the sideline? – he ended up eating two time outs as it was
* Brady talking to Lewis was good after his QB sneak
* They saw something in film that told them to do the quarterback sneak over the left side
* I was wrong about Danny Woodhead coming up big – BJGE did though
* Woodhead fumble goes back to what we have talked about all year – no real return game
* Crazy to see Brady’s legs come over his head on the QB sneak for a TD
* 4th and one for a TD was like Bart Starr in the Ice Bowl
* The interception by Brady after the Brandon Spikes turnover was one of the worst plays in Patriot history

DEFENSE

* The interception by Brandon Spikes was his biggest highlight since his porn flick
* Big Vince Wilfork came up big – three tackles for a loss
* The Ravens running game found something over their right side
* Someone please explain how Julian Edelman was one-on-one with Anquan Bolden at the end of the game?
* Brandon Spikes – 9 tackles / one insane interception (which Brady wasted) and lot of energy
* Jerod Mayo was extremely average again

RAVENS

* Joe Flacco going with the Serpico look
* Joe Flacco threw a more confident pass than Brady
* I said the Ravens would go deep on the first play but instead they went with the run that Ray Rice scored on in their last playoff game in 2009

OFFICIALS

* I was shocked the NFL didn’t take a look at the Raven’s TD – drop at the end of the game – very close to two feet down.
* Officials put the flags away

OTHER

* Anyone who is over 50 turned the TV when Steven Tyler started signing the National Anthem
* Is that they best plane they could get to fly over?
* The streak of showing Robert Kraft was sustained early and often
* Edelman should be informed that on punt returns – just catch and go forward
* By the way, Rob Gronkowski’s “friend” Bibi Jones will be dancing at Alex’s in early February
* Speaking of Gronkowski – it looked like his ankle was broken
* Patriots favored by 3 over the NY Giants – any clear-headed bettor would take New York
* Drew Bledsoe went with the double kiss on Bob Kraft – his number should be retired

2. NFC Championship:

* Great, we have a rematch from the worst loss in Patriots history
* Punt returners looking for glory cost the Houston Texans and the 49ers their Super Bowl dream
* 49ers 1-13 on third down
* Vernon Davis is one selfish player – surprised Jim Haubaugh tolerates it (bad day for the Haubaugh family)
* Eli Manning 58 pass attempts – wow

3. Celtics / Bruins:

* When Milan Lucic is moving legs and arms, he is an All-Star player
* Celtics 21% complete with season
* Bruins season is 55% complete
* Celtics are 0-6 against teams over .500
* Celtics are 26th in scoring 88.9
* Celtics are tied for 18th in turnovers with 15.5 a game
* Tyler Seguin is leading the Bruins with 40 points – thus another year without a 100-point scorer
