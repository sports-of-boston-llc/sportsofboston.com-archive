]]> By: Alan https://sportsofboston.com/2012/07/26/should-the-red-sox-retire-wade-boggs-number/comment-page-1/#comment-28659 Thu, 26 Jul 2012 17:07:26 +0000 http://sportsofboston.com/?p=66829#comment-28659 I mostly agree with the Red Sox criteria. Boggs has a lot of honors and accolades, but retiring his number should not be one of them because he chose to go elsewhere. It doesn’t matter that it was the Yankees–it matters that he left the Red Sox in the middle of his career and played for many more years for other teams. Pedro should also be excluded from having his number retired for the same reason, as well as Tek, since he won’t make the HOF. There should in the end be very few numbers retired, and the criteria should be very strict. Many great players won’t make it, and that is the way it should be. You have to be very, very, very special to have your number retired.
