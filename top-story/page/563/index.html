Did Tom Brady Need the Preseason?

September 9, 2008 at 6:29am in Featured, Opinion, Patriots, Top Story
Mike writes
2 comments

Tom Brady‘s ACL tear was a freak accident (yes, I think the hit was clean), but I wonder if he was affected by not having played a preseason game.  There was the pre-existing foot injury that kept him out of action in the preseason.  Were the injuries more serious than the Patriot organization led us to believe? 

We all know Bill Belichick is elusive, and as Patriot fans, we love him for it, so this seems like a reasonable possibility.  If the injury was worse than let on and bad enough to cost him the preseason, should he have been out there to begin with in Week 1?  Was he overcompensating for the foot or was it affecting his mobility or flexibility?  If it was more hype than substance, should he not have seen at least a few plays in at least one of the games?
