Brignac is a talented player who can definitely replace Bartlett, Garza had an ERA near 4.00 when everything was said and done so Hellickson won’t be much of a dropoff, and I’d expect Wade Davis to get better too. The bullpen lost a lot of arms, but just because the guys they have back there now are unproven doesn’t mean they aren’t good. Adam Russell and Jake McGee are good relievers. While Tampa will need a lot to go right to challenge Boston or New York, I think they’re an 88 win team that will lock up the third spot easily.
