Connelly’s Top Ten: Off Week – Take Your Mother in Law Out

By Michael in Featured, Now! ⋅ October 21, 2011 at 1:46am ⋅ Post a comment

1. Off Week for the Patriots:

High points to date…

* Tom Brady is on a pace for 43 touchdown passes (don’t forget to appreciate every game – see #6 below)
* Wes Welker’s pace: 136 receptions (record: Marvin Harrison with 143) / 2,095 yards (record: Jerry Rice with 1,848)
* Tight Ends – wow! Aaron Hernandez and Rob Gronkowski on a pace for 24 touchdowns / 150 receptions
* On a pace to win 13 games despite the fact they have lost All Pro’s Jerod Mayo and Dan Koppen / Devin McCourty has gone backwards / they have no pass rush / Ochocinco has failed

2. Would you make this Red Sox trade?

Coming off the books… Potential acquisitions…
  • J.D. Drew ($14mm)
  • David Ortiz ($12.5mm)
  • Kevin Youkilis ($12mm, trade)
  • John Lackey ($8mm relief, trade)
  • Mike Cameron ($7mm)
  • Marco Scutaro ($6mm)
  • Jason Varitek ($2mm)
  • Tim Wakefield ($2mm)
  • Dan Wheeler, Matt Albers, etc.
  • Albert Pujols ($25mm)
  • Jose Reyes ($15mm)
  • Aramis Ramirez ($15mm)
  • Michael Cuddyer ($8mm)
  • Mark Buerhle ($7mm)

In the end, both sides would equal about $70mm spent and saved.

New Lineup

  1. Jose Reyes
  2. Dustin Pedroia
  3. Jacoby Ellsbury
  4. Albert Pujols
  5. Adrian Gonzalez
  6. Aramis Ramirez
  7. Michael Cuddyer
  8. Jarrod Saltlalamacchia
  9. Carl Crawford

Bench: Ryan Kalish, Josh Reddick, Ryan Lavarnway and Jed Lowrie

Rotation: Josh Beckett, John Lester, Clay Buchholz, Daniel Bard, Mark Buerhle

3. College Football Weekend Preview:

* As I noted on Monday – Michigan State players William Gholston and Marcus Rush should be suspended for their behavior Saturday against Michigan – the former was suspended the latter has not.
* I’m a huge LSU fan, but it is becoming very difficult to root for them with so many punks on the team. To start the season, the team’s two best players (Jordan Jefferson and Russell Shepard) were suspended, and now Tyrann Mathieu, Spencer Ware, and Tharold Simon have reportedly tested positive for weed despite the fact that their team is No. 1 and has a chance to do something special.
* Games of the week: Michigan State vs. Wisconsin (Badgers win) / Stanford vs. Washington (Cardinals win) / Penn State vs. Northwestern (blow out by Wildcats by at least 10-14 points)
* Boston College will fall to 1-6 this weekend and might only win one more game this year

4. Interesting:

During the offseason, both the Bruins and the Patriots had glaring weaknesses (power play and pass rush) and neither did anything to address the problem.

5. Bruins talk:

* Patrice Bergeron and Milan Lucic are on pace to score 10 goals combined this season
* Tuukka Rask’s record: Before the team’s 2010 collapse against Flyers 29-14 / after 11-20
* On pace to win 35 games

6. Appreciate Tom Brady:

Remember these guys?

* Joe Kapp: 3 touchdowns to 17 interceptions
* Tom Hodson: 7 touchdowns to 11 interceptions
* Marc Wilson: 7 touchdowns to 16 interceptions
* Scott Secules: 2 touchdowns to 9 interceptions

7. Amazing Stats:

Patriots haven’t lost at home in the regular season since 2008 (20-0) / they haven’t won at home in the playoffs since 2007 (0-2)

8. Old School:

Which team was more despicable: 2001 Red Sox or 2011 Red Sox?

2001 Team: Carl Everett (Psycho), Izzy Alacantra (Psycho II), Uggie Urbina (didn’t he kill someone?), Manny Ramirez (drug user that shut it down), Pedro (shut it down for the season), Jose Offerman (bad guy), Scott Hatteberg (union rep who couldn’t organize the team to make a 9/11 donation), Mike Lansing (bitter and whiney), Shea Hillenbrand (would challenge Theo to a fight), Doug Mirabelli (one of the most disliked guys in Red Sox organization), Nomar (surly phony), Paxton Crawford (Acapulco cliff diver from his bed – drug user),

2011 Team: Josh Becket (tough guy wanna-be), Jon Lester (threw his manager under the bus – ingrate), Jason Varitek (threw his manager under the bus despite the fact that he played the underachieving “game caller”), Adrian Gonzalez (complains about schedule, injuries), Kevin Youkilis (sweats and complains), JD Drew (doesn’t play with broken finger), David Ortiz (questions his coach in public / crashes into press conference for selfish RBI return), John Lackey (further dilutes manager’s power by staring him down despite his performances)

9. Just a Little Bit Outside:

Pitchers who look like they were drinking while on the mound…

* Bill Lee: Game 7, 1975 (Has it landed yet?)
* Mike Torrez: Playoff game, 1978 (Bucky “F-ing” Dent)
* Bob Stanley / Calvin Schiraldi – Game 6, 1986 (I don’t have to get into it)
* Tim Wakefield: Game 7, 2003 (what a free ride he got for that)
* Tom Gordon: Game 4, 1998 (gives up two runs – team eliminated by Indians)
* Zane Smith: Game 1, 1995 (gives up home run to Tony Pena of Cleveland)
* Roger Clemens: Game 4, 1990 (gets tossed on purpose so he doesn’t prove he’s Rene Richards on national TV)

10. Randomocity:

* I like the ad campaign by Suffolk Downs – Hometown Favorite
* Off Week – be nice to your wife, go visit her family, do chores, buy flowers
* Don’t forget on October 7th (#3 item 3) that I predicted drinking in the dugout
* Would you trade Jose Iglesias, Josh Reddick and a lower prospect for Hanley Ramirez?
* And again why do people root against Tim Tebow? And root for Michael Vick?
* Patriots dead money this year (Miguel’s patscap): Ty Warren ($2.7mm), Tully Banta Cain ($2mm) Nick Kazcur ($875k)
