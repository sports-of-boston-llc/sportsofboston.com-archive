Connelly’s Top Ten: Patriots Put Foot Down on Ryan and Jets

By Michael in Featured, Now! ⋅ November 14, 2011 at 7:00am ⋅ Post a comment

1. Patriots:

1st QUARTER

* First plays, the Jets targeted Devin McCourty
* Chad Ochocinco responsible for another penalty with too many men in the huddle
* Spike Lee looks like he’s ready to fall asleep
* Ochocinco was wide open on the side and steps out of bounds
* UMass officials won’t be happy with Patriots safety James Ihedigbo calling his school of attendance – Zoo Mass
* Is it me or does either Danny Woodhead open up the wrong way for Brady in the flat or Brady keeps throwing behind him?
* Brady under-threw Ochocinco for what should of been a touchdown, but did result in a long gain
* Andre Carter on fire – two sacks and big jump in the air after

2nd QUARTER

* The fake end around to Wes Welker, and then the pass could have went for 30 yards if Brady puts the ball on him
* Brady has over 5 seconds and decides to throw into triple coverage to Deion Branch in the end zone
* What is the consecutive game count for television showing Bob Kraft at a game?
* Is it really a helmet-to-helmet penalty if you hit your own guy?
* Don’t like that hand-off and stretch play against fast linebackers like the Jets have
* On the safety, why did Brady rush that play? Even if its too much time it would have been a two yard penalty
* It was Wilfork’s worst game. Getting eaten up by one blocker Brandon Moore and #75 picked up a personal foul
* Chris Collinsworth really doesn’t like the Patriots

3rd QUARTER

* Tom Brady getting a little bit of happy feet recently
* After the fumble by the Jets on the punt – Patriots come out and run it right into the Jets line
* Great scramble by Brady on the TD by Gronkowski that got called back
* Chris Boomer Berman should stay away from any singing in that Camry commercial

4th QUARTER

* Love Fireman Ed biting his fingernails – punk!
* Hurry up should be featured 65% of the time
* Julian Edelman with a nice tackle
* Rob Nickovich, who I’ve been killing, looked like Teddy Bruschi

OTHER

* Kick off returns still a problem: Pats averaged 21 yards
* Brady QB rating: 118.4, Mark Sanchez: 64.7
* Gronkowski is looking like Dave Casper, Kellen Winslow and Tony Gonzalez in one
* Wow, Andre Carter with 4.5 sacks – who needs Fat Albert Haynesworth on that line
* They won with guys on defense named White, Adams, Tarpinian, Moore, Edelman

2. Around the NFL:

* I hate watching games in a dome
* In Tim Tebow’s three wins, he has completed 25 passes
* In Cam Newton’s last three losses, he has completed 66 passes
* Looks like Wild Cards could come from the AFC North – so the Pats better win the division

3. Around College Football:

* Penn State pre-game was emotional
* BCS slobs are breathing easy with Boise State and Stanford losing
* Looks like LSU will have to beat Arkansas in Baton Rouge and Georgia in SEC championship game for BCS invite
* When I was in Georgia two months ago, the entire state wanted Mark Richt to get “William Wallaced” now they are a win over Kentucky from the SEC championship
* Mike Sherman has now lost 5 games this year with the most talent in the country
* What’s Boston College doing for Luke Kuechly? He is leading the country again with 168 tackles (30 ahead of #2 tackler)
* Navy had zero completions in their victory over SMU
* After ten weeks, Navy has had 24 penalties (while Florida has 83 penalties)

4. Bruins:

* Tyler Seguin, Nathan Horton and Milan Lucic are shooting 22% (22-101)
* Will Tuukka Rask be made available, as mentioned in the Globe? If so at Providence Anton Khudobin is 6-6 with 2.81 GAA
* Last five-game (plus) winning streak was February 17th last year when they won 7 in a row
* Lucic will get fined for the hit on Ryan Miller
* Hearing Tyler Seguin is getting an attitude around the Boston Garden

5. Red Sox:

With Larry Lucchino in charge of players like David Ortiz, Josh Beckett, Kevin Youkilis, and Jon Lester… they are a very unlikable team.

6. Remember the good ole days with Kevin McHale and Larry Bird:

12/9/84: Larry Bird – 48 points
1/27/85: Larry Bird – 48 points
3/3/85: Kevin McHale – 56 points
3/12/85: Larry Bird – 60 points
3/17/85: Larry Bird – 48 points

7. Old School:

Harvard Football won its 14th Ivy Championship (Coach Tim Murphy is underrated). Columbia hasn’t won since 1961.

8. Amazing Stats:

Look back at Jonathan Papelbon’s place in Boston Red Sox history…

* Drafted in the 4th round in 2003
* Postseason stats: 1.00 ERA / 0.815 WHIP / 27 innings – 0 home runs
* 3rd in Red Sox appearances (396) as a pitcher behind Tim Wakefield (590) and Bob Stanley (637)
* 1st in saves with 219
* 38th in Red Sox strikeouts 509
* Best Irish step dancer out of the bullpen

9. Just a Little Bit Outside:

Updated odds for championships…

* Patriots: 4 to 1 – behind Green Bay
* Red Sox: 5 to 2 – tied with Phillies as favorite
* Celtics: 8 to 1 – tied with Dallas for fifth most likely to win
* Bruins: 15 to 1 – 10th to win it (good bet right now)

10. Randomocity:

* Lazy country – If someone doesn’t vote for three straight years, they should have to lose the right and then have to earn it back
* Just a question about the goof ball caddy Stevie Williams – is “black ass” a racial slur? If he said “fat ass” would that be discrimination against fat people?
* In light of the Penn State tragedy – google USA Swimming, where almost 50 coaches have been banned fox sexual abuse
* Does A-Rod get 3,000 hits? He’s at 2,775
* Interesting that Greg Bedard of the Globe called Logan Mankins the most over-rated player on the team
* Has anyone ever seen Greg Harris and weird Al Yankovic at the same time?
