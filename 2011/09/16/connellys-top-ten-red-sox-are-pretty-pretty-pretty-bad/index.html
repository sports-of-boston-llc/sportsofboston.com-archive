Connelly’s Top Ten: Red Sox Are Pretty, Pretty, Pretty – BAD!

By Michael in Featured, Now! ⋅ September 16, 2011 at 10:28am ⋅ Post a comment

1. Red Sox – not fun, not good, no chance:

* This collection of pitchers from Thursday night’s game remind you of the 1970 Orioles? Kyle Weiland (7.58), Trevor Miller (3.86), Scott Atchinson (4.50), Fred Morales (3.77), Matt Albers (4.95), Andrew Miller (5.43), Michael Bowden (4.76)

* Can people stop calling Texas pitchers tough – I’m sick of this stick-out-their-chest crazy competitor pitchers reputation who don’t answer the call

* Marco Scutaro on fire in September: .429 with 16 RBI

2. Patriots – Chargers preview:

* How many of the 68,000 tailgating fans are going to grab a beer and say – “Tom Brady told me to drink early and often”

* Loved Chuck Muncie’s goggles

* Speaking of Chargers: Dan Fouts = Kiki Vandeweigh

3. First Game For Chad Ochocinco:

The WR is off to a tough start with his new team. How did other notable wide receivers do with their new team in the first game?

4. Bruins 2011 Winning percentage:

Eighteen times, the Bruins had a higher regular season winning percentage than last year but did not win the Stanley Cup. In the years they won the Cup – 2011 was the lowest winning percentage at .628.

5. Belichick special – A Football Life:

* If they didn’t edit one bit of the raw footage they obtained, I could have watched that for 12 hours straight

* Love him getting into it with Derrick Mason

* He seems disinterested in dialogue with Robert Kraft

* I like when he had to set Brady straight that he wasn’t hitting his receivers right in the hands

* Great job by NFL films to take his warnings prior to the Jets game and then show the plays the following day that Belichick had warned about 12 hours earlier.

7.  Amazing Stats:

Rich Garces is listed at 250 pounds on BaseballReference.com.

8. Old School:

On November 3rd, 1974, Patriots Mack Herron outplayed OJ Simpson:

  • Herron: 146 total yards, 3 TD
  • The 2-Time Killer: 104 total yards, 1 TD

9. Just a Little Bit Outside:

Tommy gets a Tommy point for being brash, even back in 1964.

10. Randomocity

* Five players whose name started with Z played for the Pawtucket Red Sox: Eddie Zambrano, Charlie Zink, Alan Zinter, Julio Zuleta, Bob Zupic

* I’m going back to regular Doritos from Cool Ranch

* Florida State vs. Oklahoma this weekend – both teams bore me. Look out for Wisconsin this year with Russell Wilson at QB

* Speaking of Pawtucket – Okajima is 8-1 with a 2.29 ERA

* With Marchand signed for two more years, the Sedin brothers had decided to wear an Otis Sistruck neck brace to protect multiple jabs to the face

* Movie 10: under-rated
