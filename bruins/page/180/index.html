Zdeno Chara a Finalist for Norris Trophy

April 24, 2009 at 6:03am in Bruins, Featured

Zdeno Chara of the Boston Bruins, Mike Green of the Washington Capitals and Nicklas Lidstrom of the Detroit Red Wings are finalists for the Norris Trophy as the NHL’s top defenseman.

The league announced the finalists Thursday and the winner will be revealed June 18. Lidstrom is seeking his seventh Norris and fourth in a row.

The 6’9″ defenseman finished with 19 goals and 31 assists for 50 points this season for the B’s.

Lucic Suspended for Game 3

April 19, 2009 at 8:44pm in Bruins
Boston Bot writes
Add a comment

Boston left winger Milan Lucic was suspended for a game by the NHL on Sunday for a blow to the head on Montreal’s Maxim Lapierre in the Bruins’ Game 2 victory Saturday night in Boston. Lucic was assessed a match penalty for high-sticking with 4:32 left in the game.

“While it is unclear whether Lucic’s glove or stick makes contact with Lapierre, what is clear is that he delivered a reckless and forceful blow to the head of his opponent,” said Colin Campbell, the NHL’s senior executive vice president of hockey operations.”

As a result, Lucic will miss Monday’s Game 3 against the Canadiens.
